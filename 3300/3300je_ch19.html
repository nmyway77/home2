Unit 34 · 분사의 종류와 용법종류·한정·서술·감정분사·동명사구별
Unit 34 · 분사의 종류와 용법

A. 분사의 종류

1. 현재분사 (~ing) — ‘~하고 있는, ~하는’ 진행·능동. (The snow on the roof is beginning to melt. / I found a big melting ice.)
2. 과거분사 (p.p.) — ‘~한, ~된, ~당한’ 완료·수동. (This house was built by my father. / There were a lot of fallen leaves.)

B. 한정적 용법

명사의 앞이나 뒤에서 명사를 수식한다.
Look at the singing girl. = Look at the girl who is singing.
Look at the girl singing a beautiful song. = … the girl who is singing a beautiful song.
cf) falling leaves = leaves which are falling / fallen leaves = leaves which have fallen / a stolen letter = a letter which was stolen

C. 서술적 용법

1. 주격보어 — He stood reading a newspaper. / She looked relieved to hear that.
2. 목적격보어 — ① 지각동사+목적어+분사 : I saw him running. / He smelled something burning. / I heard my name called. ② have/get+목적어+과거분사 : I had my hair dyed(사역). / I had my left thumb broken(수동). / I have a title already chosen(완료).
cf) 목적어와 목적보어가 능동 관계면 동사원형(The officer had him open …) 또는 to부정사(I got him to fix my car).

D. 감정동사의 현재분사와 과거분사

사람이 감정을 느끼면 과거분사, 사물·사람이 감정을 일으키면 현재분사.
bored / boring지루한 / 지루하게 하는 excited / exciting흥분한 / 흥분시키는 interested / interesting흥미를 가진 / 흥미 있는 surprised / surprising놀란 / 놀라운 disappointed / disappointing실망한 / 실망스러운 tired / tiring피로한 / 지치게 하는
I was bored to death then. The lecture was really boring. / I was surprised at the news. The news was surprising.

E. 동명사와 현재분사의 구별

1. 동명사는 ‘~하는 것, ~하기 위한’ 의미. (Drawing cartoons is my hobby. / a sleeping bag = a bag for sleeping / a dining room = a room for dining)
2. 현재분사는 ‘~하고 있는, ~하게 하는’ 의미. (I am studying math. / Look at the girl drawing cartoons. / I heard very shocking news.)

Unit 35 · 분사 구문만들기·종류·with·독립분사구문
Unit 35 · 분사 구문

A. 분사 구문 만들기

1. 접속사를 생략한다. 2. 부사절의 주어가 주절의 주어와 같으면 생략한다. 3. 부사절의 동사를 현재분사로 바꾼다. 시제가 같으면 ‘동사원형+~ing’, 부사절 시제가 주절보다 앞서면 ‘having+p.p.’
When she saw me, she smiled brightly. → Seeing me, she smiled brightly.
As I had studied hard, I won the first prize. → Having studied hard, I won the first prize.

B. 분사 구문의 종류

1. 시간 : When he saw me, he ran away. → Seeing me, he ran away.
2. 원인·이유 : Because he is young, he is reckless. → Being young, he is reckless.
3. 부대상황 : ① 동시동작 I wrote a letter, as I was listening… → I wrote a letter, (being) listening to music. ② 연속동작 A stranger came up to me, and asked… → …came up to me, asking me to help.
4. 조건 : If you turn to the left… → Turning to the left, you will find the post office.
5. 양보 : Though I admit what you said… → Admitting what you said, I still can’t believe him.

C. with + 목적어 + 분사

부대상황. 「with+목적어+현재분사」는 목적어의 능동적 행위를, 「with+목적어+과거분사」는 수동적 행위를 나타낸다.
The girl ran into the house, with tears running down her cheeks.
He sat on the sofa with his legs crossed.

D. 주의해야 할 분사 구문

1. 부정 — 분사 앞에 not이나 never. (As she didn’t know the man… → Not knowing the man, she couldn’t say a word.)
2. Being / Having been 생략 — 부사절 be동사 뒤에 분사·형용사·명사가 올 때 생략 가능. (While I was walking… → (Being) Walking …) / (When he was compared with… → (Being) Compared with …)
3. 접속사 유지 — 의미를 분명히 하려고 접속사를 남길 수 있다. (While she was cooking… → While cooking, she listened to the radio.)

E. 독립 분사 구문

1. 독립 분사 구문 — 부사절 주어와 주절 주어가 다르면 부사절 주어를 그대로 쓴다. (As it was too cold… → It being too cold, he had to wear the fur coat. / If the weather permits… → Weather permitting, we will go hiking.)
2. 무인칭 독립 분사 구문 — 부사절 주어가 you, we, they, people 등 일반인이면 주어를 생략. (If we speak generally… → Generally speaking … / If we judge from… → Judging from …)
cf) generally speaking(일반적으로 말해), strictly speaking(엄격히 말해), frankly speaking(솔직히 말해), considering(~을 고려하면), judging from(~으로 판단할 때), compared with(~와 비교하면)
