  9. more important - most important — 3음절

Chapter VI · Unit 13 Forms of Comparison Sentences제6장 · Unit 13 비교의 형식
13-A1
원급 비교
EX
A-1. 비슷한 뜻이 되도록 한 문장으로 만들 때 빈칸을 채우세요.
  1. I've lived here for two years. My cat has lived here for two years. → My cat has lived here long .
  2. I am 178 cm tall. He is 185 cm tall. → I am tall he.
  3. I know a little about computers. But my brother knows more about them. → I do know about computers much my brother.
  4. I didn't work very hard. Jane worked very hard. → I didn't work hard Jane.
  5. A cat is not very big. A lion is big. → A cat is big a lion.
  6. The house was big, but I expected that the house was bigger than that. → The house was not as big I .
  7. He is tall, but I thought he would be taller than that. → He is not so tall I he would be.
13-A1번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
as + 원급 + as(~만큼 …한), 부정은 not as/so + 원급 + as(~만큼 …하지 못한).
  1. as / as / I — as long as I (have/do)
  2. not / so(as) / as — not so tall as he
  3. not / so(as) / as — not so much as my brother
  4. so(as) / as — not so hard as Jane
  5. not / so(as) / as — not so big as a lion
  6. as / expected — not as big as I expected
  7. as / thought — not so tall as I thought
13-A2
as~as
EX
A-2. 괄호 속 단어들을 사용하여 문장을 완성하세요.
  1. 가능한 한 일찍 집으로 돌아오너라. → Come back home . (early, as, possible, as)
  2. 그는 가능한 한 느리게 걸었다. → He walked . (could, slowly, as, he, as)
  3. 그는 여우처럼 간사하다. → He is . (as, cunning, a fox, as)
13-A2번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
as + 원급 + as possible = as + 원급 + as one can(가능한 한 ~하게). 직유: as ~ as a fox.
  1. as early as possible — 가능한 한 일찍
  2. as slowly as he could — 가능한 한 느리게
  3. as cunning as a fox — 여우처럼 간사한
13-B1
비교급 전환
EX
B-1. 다음 문장을 같은 뜻의 비교급 문장으로 바꾸세요.
  1. Ji-na is not as tall as Jeong-won. → Jeong-won is Ji-na.
  2. I like history. I don't like math very much. → I like history .
  3. I am not as popular as his teacher. → His teacher is .
  4. You are not so pretty as Jane. → Jane is you.
  5. This quiz is not as difficult as that one. → That quiz is this one.
13-B1번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
「A is not as ~ as B」 = 「B is 비교급 than A」로 전환.
  1. taller than — 정운이가 더 크다
  2. more than math / better than math — 역사를 더 좋아한다
  3. more popular than me — 그의 선생님이 더 인기 있다
  4. prettier than — Jane이 더 예쁘다
  5. more difficult than — 저 퀴즈가 더 어렵다
13-B2
점점·the비교급
EX
B-2. 다음 우리말을 영어로 고칠 때 빈칸을 알맞게 채우세요.
  1. 그의 몸 상태가 점점 더 나빠졌다. → His condition got and .
  2. 그는 점점 공부를 덜 했고, 그의 성적은 점점 더 나빠졌다. → He studied and and his grades became worse and worse.
  3. 나는 긴장하면 할수록 더 많은 실수를 한다. → nervous I get, mistakes I make.
  4. 공부를 열심히 할수록 더 높은 점수를 받는다. → you study, score you get.
13-B2번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
비교급 and 비교급 = 점점 더 ~한. The 비교급 ~, the 비교급 … = ~할수록 더 …하다.
  1. worse and worse — 점점 더 나쁜
  2. less and less — 점점 덜
  3. The more / the more — 긴장할수록 실수가 많아짐
  4. The harder / the higher — 열심히 할수록 점수가 높아짐
13-C1
최상급 전환
EX
C-1. 다음 문장을 최상급의 의미가 되도록 빈칸을 채우세요.
  1. The Pacific Ocean is the biggest ocean in the world.
    = (other) in the world is the Pacific Ocean.
    = (other) in the world is the Pacific Ocean.
    = The Pacific Ocean is ocean in the world.
    = The Pacific Ocean is oceans in the world.
  2. She is the smartest student in my school.
    = (other) in my school is her.
    = (other) in my school is her.
    = She is student in my school.
    = She is students in my school.
13-C1번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
최상급 = No (other) ~ as 원급 as = No (other) ~ 비교급 than = 비교급 than any other 단수명사 = 비교급 than all the other 복수명사.
  1. No / ocean / as big as — No other ocean is as big as ~
  2. No / ocean / bigger than — No other ocean is bigger than ~
  3. bigger than any other — than any other ocean
  4. bigger than all the other — than all the other oceans
  5. No / student / as smart as
  6. No / student / smarter than
  7. smarter than any other — than any other student
  8. smarter than all the other — than all the other students
13-C2
최상급 배열
EX
C-2. 괄호 안의 단어들을 어법에 맞게 배열하여 문장을 완성하세요.
  1. 내가 가장 좋아하는 것은 야구다. → baseball. (nothing, more, I, there's, like, than)
  2. James는 우리 학교에서 최고의 선수 중 한명이다. → James is in my school. (the, one, of, best, players)
  3. 그녀가 가장 잘 하는 것은 그림을 그리는 것이다. → painting. (than, there's, she, better, can, do, nothing)
  4. 하나는 한국에서 가장 아름다운 여배우 중 하나다. → Hana is in Korea. (one, actresses, the, of, most, beautiful)
13-C2번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
There's nothing ~ 비교급 than(…보다 더 ~한 것은 없다), one of the 최상급 + 복수명사(가장 ~한 것 중 하나).
  1. There's nothing I like more than — 내가 가장 좋아하는
  2. one of the best players — 최고 선수 중 하나
  3. There's nothing she can do better than — 그녀가 가장 잘하는
  4. one of the most beautiful actresses — 가장 아름다운 여배우 중 하나
13-D
비교·최상급 강조
EX
D. 주어진 철자로 시작하는 말로 비교급·최상급 문장을 강조해 보세요.
  1. He is the tallest student in my class. (m~)
  2. You are the cleverest student of all the students. (v~)
  3. This house is more expensive than mine. (a~)
  4. Jane is more beautiful than me. (e~)
13-D번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
비교급 강조 much/still/even/far/a lot(훨씬). 최상급 강조 the very / much the / by far the(단연코).
  1. much — much the tallest(최상급 강조)
  2. very — the very cleverest(최상급 강조)
  3. a lot — a lot more expensive(비교급 강조)
  4. even — even more beautiful(비교급 강조)

① Ken is a better driver than Tom. ② You can do it better. ③ To become a better writer, you should read a lot. ④ It is better to stay. ⑤ Better days are coming.
16번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
② better = well
②의 better는 부사 well의 비교급. 나머지는 형용사 good의 비교급.

21. 괄호 안의 단어를 사용하여 문장을 완성하세요.
• 그것은 내가 지금까지 들었던 것 중 가장 이상한 사건이다.
= That's ________. (I, the, have, strangest, of, ever, heard, accident)
21번 정답 & 해설 펼치기

the strangest accident I have ever heard of
최상급 + 명사 + (that) 주어 + have ever p.p. = 지금까지 …한 것 중 가장 ~한.

23. 대화의 빈칸에 들어갈 표현 중 적절하지 않은 것을 고르세요.
A: Which movie do you like best?
B: The Lord of the Rings. What about you?
A: Me, too. ________.
① I like it the best ② Nothing can be better than it ③ There's nothing I like more than it ④ I like it more than any other movies ⑤ No movie is better than it
23번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
④ any other movies → any other movie
비교급 + than any other + 단수명사가 원칙. movies(복수)는 틀리다.
