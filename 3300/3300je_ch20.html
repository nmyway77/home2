Unit 36 · 주어와 동사의 일치

A. A and B

1. 'a/the+명사+and+명사'는 단수 취급한다.
The dramatist and actor is young.그 극작가이자 배우는 젊다.A needle and thread was on the sheet.실을 꿴 바늘이 시트 위에 있었다.A watch and chain was found on a subway.줄이 있는 시계가 지하철에서 발견됐다.
2. 'a/the+명사+and+a/the+명사'는 복수 취급한다.
The dramatist and the actor are all men.극작가와 배우는 모두 남자이다.
3. 'and'로 연결되는 두 요소가 전체적으로 한 개념일 때는 단수 취급한다.
Early to bed and early to rise makes you healthy, wealthy and wise.일찍 자고 일찍 일어나는 것은 당신을 건강하게, 부유하게, 현명하게 만든다.cf) 별개의 개념일 때는 복수 취급 — To know something and to practice it are two different things.
4. 'a+단수명사+and a half+단수동사' = 'one and a half+복수명사+복수동사'
A year and a half has gone by. = One and a half years have gone by.

B. all, most, half, etc.

1. all/most/half/some/part/분수/a lot/lots/plenty of는 뒤따라오는 명사의 수에 따라 단·복수가 결정된다.
Most of the apples are very sweet. / Most of the apple is bad.
Two-thirds of the surface of the planet is covered with water. / Two-thirds of these oranges are rotten.
Plenty of women are playing golf. / Plenty of money was stolen last night.
2. 'one of+복수명사'는 단수 취급한다.
One of my favorite subjects is math.
3. 'a number of ~'는 복수 취급, 'the number of ~'는 단수 취급한다.
A number of restaurants were open.많은 식당들이 열었다.The number of cars is decreasing.차들의 수가 감소하고 있다.

C. every, each, either of, etc.

1. every, each, either of는 단수 취급한다.
Every student was present. / Each of us was against the bill. / Either of the plans is right.
2. all이 '모든 것, ~하는 모든 것'으로 해석되면 단수 취급, '모든 사람'으로 해석되면 복수 취급한다.
All is silent tonight. / All look happy.
3. 「either A or B, neither A nor B, Not A but B」의 수는 동사에 가까운 명사의 수에 따른다.
Either they or she has the will. / Neither you nor I am wrong. / Not he but I have the right to choose the menu.
4. 「not only A but also B = B as well as A」의 동사의 수는 B에 일치한다.
Not only my brothers but also my sister is a soldier. = My sister as well as my brothers is a soldier.

D. 주의해야 할 수의 일치

Mathematics is hard to master. (학문·학과는 단수 취급)Little Women is a feminine novel. (복수형으로 된 서적·신문·잡지명은 단수 취급)The New York Times is one of the most influential newspapers in America.
Five years is a long time to wait for you. (시간)Ten miles is too far for you to walk a day. (거리)One hundred kilograms is too heavy to lift. (무게)One hundred dollars is too much for the shoes. (금액)What is important is to keep learning. ('~하는 것'의 what은 단수)Enough is as good as a feast. (enough가 양일 때 단수)If enough are sold, a better version will be made. (enough가 수일 때 복수)It is you who are to blame for the accident. (관계대명사절의 동사는 선행사에 일치)The girl is seventeen years old. = She is a seventeen-year-old girl. (복합형용사의 명사는 단수)

EXERCISE A · Unit 36
다음 문장에서 어법상 가장 알맞은 어구를 괄호 안에서 고르세요.
1.Bread and butter (was, were) on the dish.
2.The composer and conductor (was, were) really handsome and gentle.
3.A poet and a farmer (was, were) talking with each other.
4.A needle and thread (is, are) placed in the cradle.
5.To preach and to practice (is, are) two different things.
6.Slow and steady (win, wins) the race.
7.A day and a half (has, have) passed since the child was kidnapped.
8.One and a half weeks (has, have) passed since his son was missing.
9.My bow and arrow (is, are) over there.
10.Nations and cultures that (has, have) long histories have something in common.
11.All work and no play (make, makes) Jack a dull boy.
12.Romeo and Juliet (is, are) a tragedy.
13.Playing squash and listening to music (is, are) her hobbies.
14.I have lived here for one and a half (year, years).
정답 & 해설 보기
1. was — 전체가 한 개념(버터 바른 빵) → 단수
2. was — 관사 하나 — 작곡가이자 지휘자(동일인) → 단수
3. were — a poet and a farmer — 별개의 두 사람 → 복수
4. is — 실을 꿴 바늘(a needle and thread) → 단수
5. are — 설교하는 것과 실천하는 것 — 별개 개념 → 복수
6. wins — 속담·한 개념(느리고 착실한 것) → 단수
7. has — a day and a half → 단수
8. have — one and a half + 복수명사(weeks) + 복수동사 → have
9. is — 활과 화살 한 세트(a bow and arrow) → 단수
10. have — 선행사 Nations and cultures(복수) → have
11. makes — 속담·한 개념(공부만 하고 놀지 않는 것) → 단수
12. is — 작품명(로미오와 줄리엣) → 단수
13. are — 스쿼시와 음악 감상 — 별개의 두 활동, 보어 hobbies도 복수 → are
14. years — one and a half + 복수명사 → years

밑줄 친 ①~⑤ 중에서 어법상 어색한 것을 고르세요.
①What he insisted ②were considered ③positively. However, it is also true that the circumstances Korea ④is facing today ⑤are not all that easy.
정답 & 해설 보기
정답 ②
주어 "What he insisted"(관계대명사 what절, 단수 취급) → ②were → was considered. ④ 주어 Korea(단수) → is 적절, ⑤ 주어 the circumstances(복수) → are 적절.

기출
다음 글을 읽고 어법상 어색한 곳을 골라 바르게 고치세요.
It's he and I that am responsible for the accident.
정답 & 해설 보기
정답 : am → are
강조구문 It is ~ that …에서 that절의 동사는 선행사의 수에 일치. 선행사 "he and I"(복수) → are.

기출
다음 중 어법상 어색한 문장을 고르세요.
①Not only Jane but also I are hungry.
②Mathematics is my favorite subject.
③Susan as well as you is a cute girl.
④A teacher and musician is supposed to sing for my birthday.
⑤Fifty miles is a long distance for us to walk in a day.
정답 & 해설 보기
정답 ①
① "Not only A but also B"는 B(I)에 동사 일치 → are → am hungry. ③ A as well as B는 A(Susan)에 일치 → is, ④ 관사 하나(교사이자 음악가=동일인) → is, ⑤ 거리 → is. ②~⑤ 적절.
