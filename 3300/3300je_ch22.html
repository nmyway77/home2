Unit 39 · 도치

A. 문법상의 습관적인 도치

1. 「There/Here+동사+주어」
There was a book on the desk. / Here comes your teacher.
cf) Here he comes. — 주어가 대명사일 경우에는 동사와 주어의 위치가 바뀌지 않는다.
2. 「So/Neither/Nor+동사+주어」
I like green tea. - So do I. / I don't like coffee. - Neither/Nor do I.
cf) '정말 그래.'의 의미일 때는 「So+주어+동사」 — I love Tom. - So you do.
3. 의문문 — 「의문사+조동사+주어+동사」 / 「의문사+be동사+주어」 / 「조동사+주어+동사」
4. 감탄문 — 「How+형용사+주어+동사」 / 「What+(a/an)+형용사+명사+주어+동사」
How beautiful these flowers are! / What a nice guy he is!
5. 기원문 — 「May+주어+동사」
May he rest in peace! / May you live long!

B. 강조를 위한 도치

1. 보어의 도치 — 「보어+동사+주어」 (주어가 대명사일 때는 「보어+주어+동사」)
The sound of the bell grew faint. → Faint grew the sound of the bell.
2. 목적어의 도치 — 「목적어+주어+동사」 (부정어 포함 시 「부정어+목적어+조동사+주어+동사」)
They can do all of them. → All of them they can do.
Not a word did she say.
3. 부사의 도치 — 「부사+동사+주어」 (주어가 대명사일 때는 「부사+주어+동사」)
The rain came down. → Down came the rain. / Down she fell.
4. 부정어의 도치 — 「부정어구+조동사+주어+동사」
She had no sooner seen me than she ran away. → No sooner had she seen me than she ran away.
We do not realize the value of our health until we lose it. → Not until we lose our health do we realize its value.

Unit 40 · 강조

1. 「It is/was ~ that …」 구문

I met Susan in the park last night.
→ It was I that(who) met Susan in the park last night. (I를 강조)
→ It was Susan that(whom) I met in the park last night. (Susan을 강조)
→ It was in the park that(where) I met Susan last night. (in the park를 강조)
→ It was last night that(when) I met Susan in the park. (last night을 강조)
의문사를 강조할 때는 평서문의 형태로 바꾸고 다시 의문문의 형태로 — 「의문사+is/was it that ~?」
Who broke this television set? → Who was it that broke this television set?
Why did he leave Busan? → Why was it that he left Busan?
의문사가 없는 의문문은 「Is/Was it ~ that …?」으로 강조한다.
Did you visit the museum yesterday? → Was it you that visited the museum yesterday? / Was it the museum that you visited yesterday? / Was it yesterday that you visited the museum?

2. 동사의 강조 — 조동사 do

I never did go there. / Do be quiet.

3. 비교급의 강조 — far, much, still, even, a lot

The sun is far larger than the moon.

4. 명사의 강조 — the very (바로 그)

That is the very book I wanted.

5. 의문사의 강조 — ever, on earth, in the world (도대체)

What on earth are you doing?

6. 부정의 강조 — 부정어 뒤에 at all, in the least

I couldn't solve the problem at all.

Unit 41 · 생략과 평형구조

A. 생략

1. 공통되는 요소의 생략
To err is human and to forgive is divine. → To err is human and to forgive divine.
A bird in the hand is worth two birds in the bush. → A bird in the hand is worth two in the bush.
You may go home if you want to go home. → You may go home if you want to.
2. 접속사가 이끄는 부사절에서 '주어+be동사'의 생략
Don't make a noise while you are eating. → Don't make a noise while eating.
You may leave now, if you are busy. → You may leave now, if busy.
3. 감탄문에서 '주어+be동사'의 생략
What shocking news it is! → What shocking news!
4. 비교구문에서의 생략
I studied harder than I studied before. → I studied harder than before.
She is as tall as I am. → She is as tall as I.
5. 목적격 관계대명사의 생략
The girl whom I love now is Ji-su. → The girl I love now is Ji-su.
6. '관계대명사+be동사+분사'에서 '관계대명사+be동사'의 생략
What is the name of the game which is played by old people? → What is the name of the game played by old people?
7. 광고·게시물에서의 생략
No parking is allowed. → No parking.

B. 평형구조

1. 등위접속사 and, but, or 등으로 연결되는 어구는 서로 동일한 형태를 취해야 한다.
I would listen to music, go to the movies, and play computer games.
We spent a lot of time chatting and exchanging information by computer.
2. 상관접속사 「both A and B」, 「neither A nor B」 등에서 A, B는 동일한 형태를 취해야 한다.
The movie was both funny and exciting. / He is neither smart nor rich.

③ⓒ - It was a beautiful bird that you saw in the garden yesterday.
④ⓓ - It was in the garden where you saw a beautiful bird yesterday.
⑤ⓔ - It was yesterday when you saw a beautiful bird in the garden.
정답 & 해설 보기
정답 ②
It is ~ that 강조구문으로는 동사(saw)를 강조할 수 없다. ②는 비문. 주어·목적어·부사(구)는 강조 가능하므로 ①③④⑤는 적절.

①She doesn't like to watch TV, and neither do I.
②I do feel better today!
③He did play basketball very well when he was my age.
④She thought I didn't like her, but I did like her.
⑤You do hate my hair style.

정답 ①
① neither do I의 do는 앞 동사를 대신하는 대동사. ②③④⑤는 모두 동사를 강조하는 강조의 do.

When they are praised, Koreans usually say that they have done nothing to deserve such praise.

부사절의 주어가 주절의 주어(Koreans)와 일치할 때 「주어+be동사」 생략 가능 → When (they are) praised. 생략 부분 = they are.

We had a wonderful time ①riding about, ②singing folk songs, ③and talked about our future dreams ④until ⑤late at night.

밑줄 친 did와 같은 용법으로 쓰인 것을 고르세요.
A: I guess the guide didn't even take you to Gyeongju.
B: We did go there! It was full of wonderful Korean historical sites.
①Did you go shopping yesterday?
②I did watch the game.
③You did not even answer my question.
④That will do.
⑤Would you do me a favor?

정답 ②
예문의 did는 동사(go) 강조. ② watch를 강조하는 동일 용법. ① 의문 조동사, ③ 부정 조동사, ④⑤ 일반동사 do.

Light travels much faster than sound.

②far
③still
④very

비교급(faster)을 강조하는 부사는 a lot, far, still, even, much. very는 비교급을 강조할 수 없다(원급 강조).
