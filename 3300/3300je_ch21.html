Unit 37 · 시제의 일치

A. 시제 일치의 원칙

1. 주절의 시제가 현재·현재완료·미래이면 종속절은 현재·미래·현재완료·과거 등이 자유롭게 올 수 있다.
He says (that) he was busy yesterday.
He has said (that) he will be busy tomorrow.
He will say (that) he has been busy recently.
2. 주절의 시제가 과거이면 종속절의 시제는 과거나 과거완료가 되어야 한다.
He said (that) he was busy yesterday.
He said (that) he had been busy before.
3. 주절의 동사가 현재에서 과거로 바뀌면 종속절의 동사는 현재·미래는 과거로, 현재완료·과거는 과거완료로 바뀐다.
He says he is busy. → He said he was busy.
He says he will be busy. → He said he would be busy.
He says he has been busy. → He said he had been busy.
He says he was busy. → He said he had been busy.

B. 시제 일치의 예외

1. 현재의 습관·사실·특성·사회적 통념은 항상 현재시제를 쓴다.
Did you know swallows migrate to Korea in April?
2. 불변의 진리나 속담은 항상 현재시제를 쓴다.
We were taught that the square of 10 is 100.
3. 역사적 사실은 항상 과거시제를 쓴다.
He said that America was discovered by Columbus in 1492.
4. 비교급의 than이나 as 등에 이어지는 구문의 시제는 주절의 시제와 상관없다.
He was healthier than he is now.
5. 종속절의 가정법 시제는 변하지 않는다.
She said, "If I were a bird, I could fly." → She said that if she were a bird, she could fly.
6. 조동사 must, need, ought to, used to, had better의 시제는 변하지 않는다.
He said, "You must go there." → He said that I must go there.

Unit 38 · 화법 전환

1. 평서문의 화법 전환

He said to me, "I will tell you the truth tonight." → He told me that he would tell me the truth that night.
① 전달동사 say는 say로, say to는 tell로, said는 said로, said to는 told로 바꾼다.
② that을 써서 두 문장을 연결한다.
③ that절의 주어·목적어·보어로 쓰인 인칭대명사는 전달하는 사람의 입장으로 바꾼다.
④ 시제의 일치 원칙에 따라 시제를 바꾼다.
⑤ 지시대명사나 부사(구)는 전달하는 사람의 입장으로 바꾼다.
this → that, now → then, here → there, today → that day
last night → the previous night / the night before
tomorrow → the following day / the next day
yesterday → the previous day / the day before
단, 위의 전환은 절대적이지 않으므로 상황에 맞게 전환한다.
This morning she said to me, "I'll meet him tomorrow evening." → This morning she told me that she would meet him tomorrow evening.

2. 의문문의 화법 전환

① 의문사가 없는 일반의문문은 전달동사를 ask로 바꾸고 if나 whether로 두 문장을 연결하고 '주어+동사'의 어순으로 바꾼다.
She said to me, "Are you free?" → She asked me if/whether I was free.
② 의문사가 있는 의문문은 전달동사를 ask로 바꾸고 의문사로 두 문장을 연결하고 '주어+동사'의 어순으로 바꾼다.
He said to her, "When is your birthday?" → He asked her when her birthday was.
③ 의문사가 주어인 의문문은 그대로 '의문사+동사 ~'의 어순이다.
He said to me, "Who did it?" → He asked me who had done it.

3. 명령문의 화법 전환

전달동사를 명령문의 성격에 따라 tell, advise, ask로 바꾸고 동사원형을 to부정사로 바꾼다.
The teacher said to the students, "Listen to me carefully." → The teacher told the students to listen to him carefully.

평서문 화법 전환: my → his(전달자 입장), is → was(시제 일치), that절로 연결. = He said quietly that his wife was not at home.
12
기출
다음 문장 중 어법상 그 형태가 옳지 않은 것을 고르세요.
①Two and seven makes nine.
②I went to bed at nine last night.
③King Se-jong had invented Han-geul.
④Water freezes at 0°C.
⑤The earth turns round the sun.
정답 & 해설 보기
정답 ③
역사적 사실은 과거시제로 표현하며 과거완료가 불필요 → had invented → invented. ① 계산(현재), ④⑤ 불변의 진리(현재) — 적절.
13
기출
아래의 문장을 올바르게 바꾼 문장을 고르세요.
Charlie said to me, "Can you sing a song for me?"
①Charlie asked me what could I sing a song for me.
②Charlie asked me can I sing a song for him.
③Charlie asked me if I could sing a song for me.
④Charlie asked me if I could sing a song for him.
⑤Charlie asked me if could I sing a song for me.
정답 & 해설 보기
정답 ④
일반의문문 → ask + if + 주어+동사 어순. you → I, can → could, me → him(전달자 입장). = Charlie asked me if I could sing a song for him.

기출
다음의 간접 화법 문장을 직접 화법으로 바꿀 때 빈칸을 채우세요.
He asked her if she wanted anything from him.
→ He said to her, " ?"
정답 & 해설 보기
정답 : Do you want anything from me
if절 일반의문문 복원: she → you, wanted → want, from him → from me. = He said to her, "Do you want anything from me?"

기출
다음 중 어법상 틀린 문장을 고르세요.
①If it snows tomorrow, we can't go climbing the mountain.
②Will you get a bottle of water when you go to the supermarket?
③She won't go on a business trip if you won't get better tomorrow.
④I don't know when she will come back.
⑤He must finish planning the project before she comes back from the trip.
정답 & 해설 보기
정답 ③
조건의 부사절에서는 미래를 현재시제로 → if you won't get better → if you don't get better. ④ know의 목적어(명사절)의 when은 미래시제 가능.

기출
두 문장의 뜻이 같도록 바꿀 때 빈칸을 채우세요.
He asked me if I could come to the party.
→ He said to me, " to the party?"
정답 & 해설 보기
정답 : Can you come
if절 일반의문문 복원: I could → Can you. = He said to me, "Can you come to the party?"
17
기출
<보기>와 같이 주어진 문장을 바꾸세요. 보기) My uncle said, "Where are you going?" → My uncle asked where I was going.
She said, "When did you write this essay?"
→ .
정답 & 해설 보기
정답 : She asked when I had written this essay.
의문사 의문문 → ask + 의문사 + 주어+동사. did write(과거) → had written(과거완료). = She asked when I had written this essay.
18
기출
다음 문장을 간접화법으로 바꿀 때 빈칸을 채우세요.
You said to me, "Who used my towel?"
→ You asked me who towel.
정답 & 해설 보기
정답 : had used my
의문사가 주어인 의문문 → 어순 유지. used(과거) → had used, my towel은 전달자 입장에서 그대로 my. = who had used my towel.
19
기출
문장을 전환한 것 중 잘못된 것을 고르세요.
①The volunteer said, "Do you want a helping hand?" → The volunteer asked if I want a helping hand.
②The police officer said, "Where were the suspects?" → The police officer asked where the suspects had been.
③She said, "May I go home?" → She asked if she might go home.
④You said, "When did you have dinner?" → You asked when I had had dinner.
⑤The magician said, "Where is the card?" → The magician asked where the card was.
정답 & 해설 보기
정답 ①
주절이 과거(asked)이므로 시제 일치 → want → wanted. = The volunteer asked if I wanted a helping hand. 나머지는 올바른 전환.

기출
다음 문장을 간접화법으로 바꾸어 쓰세요.
He said to me, "Who wrote the report for you?"
→ .
정답 & 해설 보기
정답 : He asked me who had written the report for me.
의문사가 주어인 의문문 → 어순 유지. wrote → had written, for you → for me(전달자 입장). = He asked me who had written the report for me.
21
기출
다음의 문장을 전환할 때 적당하지 않은 것을 고르세요.
①Edward said, "Can you play soccer with me?" → Edward asked if I could play soccer with him.
②A waitress said, "Do you need to eat all these things?" → A waitress asked if I needed to eat all those things.
③The clerk said, "When did the boy buy these candies?" → The clerk asked when the boy had bought those candies.
④The secretary said, "Where is the visitor?" → The secretary asked where the visitor was.
⑤Eric said to me, "Do you really want my love?" → Eric asked me if you really wanted his love.
정답 & 해설 보기
정답 ⑤
전달자 입장에서 you → I로 바꿔야 한다 → Eric asked me if I really wanted his love. 나머지 ①~④는 인칭·시제·지시어 전환이 모두 올바르다.
