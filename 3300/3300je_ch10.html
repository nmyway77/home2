Unit 18완전자동사부터 수여동사까지
A
완전자동사
다음 괄호 속에서 어법에 맞는 어구에 ○표 하세요.
1.I (arrived, arrived at) your office late.
2.Her English score (rose, raised) 10 points this semester.
3.He will (lie, lay) the key in the drawer.
4.She (lay, laid) on the sofa holding a cat.
5.Your aptitude (lays, lies) in arts.
6.The airplane (rose, raised) slowly into the air.
7.You (lied, laid) to all of us.
8.The train (departed, departed from) platform 3.
9.I (lay, laid) two cups of coffee on the table last night.
10.In this game, when you say 'No,' you will (dead, die).
정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · 어법
1.arrived at — arrive는 자동사, 장소 앞에 at이 필요하다.
2.rose — 「오르다」 자동사 rise-rose-risen.
3.lay — 목적어 the key가 있으므로 「놓다」 타동사 lay.
4.lay — 목적어가 없으므로 「눕다」 lie의 과거 lay.
5.lies — 「~에 있다」 자동사 lie.
6.rose — 「떠오르다」 자동사 rise의 과거 rose.
7.lied — 「거짓말하다」 lie-lied-lied.
8.departed from — depart는 자동사, 출발 장소 앞에 from.
9.laid — 목적어 two cups → 「놓다」 lay-laid-laid의 과거 laid.
10.die — 동사 자리이므로 동사 die (dead는 형용사).
B
불완전자동사
다음 괄호 속에서 어법에 맞는 단어에 ○표 하세요.
1.You and Jane look (serious, seriously).
2.These flowers smell (good, well).
3.It sounds (interesting, interestingly).
4.This noodle tastes (hot, hotly).
5.You should drive (careful, carefully).
6.This glass of milk went (bad, badly).
7.It was getting (cold, coldly) these days.
8.This orange tastes (terrible, terribly).
9.This cloth feels (smooth, smoothly).
10.He behaved (bad, badly).
11.He reads (good, well).
12.He stayed (awake, wake) two days in a row.
13.A cup of green tea sounds (perfect, perfectly).
14.If you walk in Busan in winter, it's difficult to keep (warm, warmly) due to cold wind.
15.Unfortunately, Edgar Allan Poe died (young, youngly).
16.I got (hurt, hurting) at work last year.
17.Though his boss entered the room, he remained (seating, seated).
18.We didn't eat the soup for 3 days, so it went (sour, sourly).
19.Pitt turned (pale, palely) at the news of the murder.
정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · 어법
1.serious — look은 불완전자동사, 보어로 형용사.
2.good — smell + 형용사 보어.
3.interesting — sound + 형용사 보어.
4.hot — taste + 형용사 보어.
5.carefully — drive는 완전자동사, 동작을 꾸미는 부사.
6.bad — go bad(상하다), 형용사 보어.
7.cold — get + 형용사 보어.
8.terrible — taste + 형용사 보어.
9.smooth — feel + 형용사 보어.
10.badly — behave는 완전자동사, 부사가 수식.
11.well — read는 완전자동사, 부사가 수식.
12.awake — stay + 형용사 보어. awake(깨어 있는)가 형용사.
13.perfect — sound + 형용사 보어.
14.warm — keep + 형용사(따뜻한 상태를 유지하다).
15.young — die + 형용사(유사보어). 「젊어서 죽다」.
16.hurt — get hurt(다치다), 과거분사 보어.
17.seated — remain + 과거분사(앉은 채로). 수동 관계.
18.sour — go sour(시어지다), 형용사 보어.
19.pale — turn + 형용사(창백해지다).
C
자동사로 착각하기 쉬운 완전타동사
다음 괄호 속에서 어법에 맞는 어구에 ○표 하세요.
1.The animal (resembled, resembled to) a tiger.
2.(Answer, Answer to) me, Ji-na.
3.She (married, married with) me last year.
4.He (stayed, stayed at) her house.
5.What are you (looking, looking at)?
6.Let's (discuss, discuss about) the matter, shall we?
7.She (entered, entered into) the coffee shop in haste.
8.I (agree, agree with) you on that point.
9.Why don't you (consider, consider about) other's feelings?
10.Did he (attend, attend at) the conference with her?
11.I was (waiting, waiting for) you.
12.Mariah Carey is said to (have inhabited, have inhabited in) this house.
13.His mother (awaited, awaited for) him for three hours.
14.When I went into the classroom, I (heard, listened) her singing a song.
정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · 어법
1.resembled — resemble은 완전타동사, to 불필요.
2.Answer — answer는 타동사, to 불필요.
3.married — marry는 타동사, with 불필요.
4.stayed at — stay는 자동사, 장소 앞에 at 필요.
5.looking at — 「보다」의 뜻은 look at.
6.discuss — discuss는 타동사, about 불필요.
7.entered — 「~에 들어가다」 enter는 타동사, into 불필요.
8.agree with — 사람과 의견 일치는 agree with.
9.consider — consider는 타동사, about 불필요.
10.attend — 「~에 참석하다」 attend는 타동사.
11.waiting for — 「~을 기다리다」 wait for.
12.have inhabited — inhabit는 타동사, in 불필요.
13.awaited — await는 타동사, for 불필요.
14.heard — 목적어 her를 직접 취하는 타동사 hear. listen은 자동사(to 필요).
D
타동사로 착각하기 쉬운 자동사
다음 괄호 속에서 어법에 맞는 어구에 ○표 하세요.
1.Your idleness (accounts, accounts for) your failure.
2.I (opposed, opposed to) his taking the position.
3.She was (waiting, waiting for) me just then.
4.We expect that casinos will (add, add to) social ills.
5.Don't (complain, complain of) my choice.
6.I (apologized at, apologized to) her as to what I have done and she accepted it.
7.She (replied, replied to) my letter.
8.You didn't (agree, agree to) my suggestion.
9.She wouldn't (agree to, agree with) him on the issue.
10.I (sympathized to, sympathized with) the woman whose kid was missing.
11.My job doesn't (interfere, interfere with) my school work.
12.We (object, object to) all the chemicals.
13.Smoking (increased, increased to) the risk of death.
14.Mr. Kim (explained, explained to) the bad effects of the greenhouse effect.
정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · 어법
1.accounts for — 「~을 설명하다」 account for.
2.opposed — oppose는 타동사, to 불필요.
3.waiting for — 「~을 기다리다」 wait for.
4.add to — 「~을 증가시키다」 add to.
5.complain of — 「~에 대해 불평하다」 complain of/about.
6.apologized to — 「~에게 사과하다」 apologize to.
7.replied to — 「~에 응답하다」 reply to.
8.agree to — 제안에 찬성은 agree to.
9.agree with — 사람과의 동의는 agree with.
10.sympathized with — 「~을 동정하다」 sympathize with.
11.interfere with — 「~에 간섭/방해하다」 interfere with.
12.object to — 「~에 반대하다」 object to.
13.increased — 목적어 the risk가 있으므로 타동사 increase(~을 증가시키다).
14.explained — 목적어를 직접 취하는 타동사 explain. explain to+사람 어순과 구분.
E
구동사
다음 괄호 속에서 어법에 맞는 어구에 ○표 하세요.
1.He put (it on, on it) slowly.
2.We wanted to carry (out the plans, out them).
3.I'll (pick you up, pick up you) at 8 o'clock.
4.Please turn (the lights off, off them).
5.He took (off it, his hat off) quickly.
6.They'd rather put (off the schedule, off it).
7.We tried to (figure out it, figure the answer out).
8.Put (them away, away them).
9.We finally (found out the technique, found out it).
10.My grandparents came to the station to (see me off, see off me).
정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · 어법
1.it on — 대명사 목적어는 「동사+대명사+부사」 어순.
2.out the plans — 명사 목적어는 부사 앞뒤 모두 가능. 대명사면 carry them out이어야 한다.
3.pick you up — 대명사 목적어는 동사와 부사 사이에.
4.the lights off — 명사는 부사 앞뒤 가능. 대명사면 turn them off.
5.his hat off — 대명사 it은 off 앞(take it off), 명사는 어순 자유.
6.off the schedule — 명사는 put off + 명사 가능. 대명사면 put it off.
7.figure the answer out — 대명사 it은 figure it out. 명사는 어순 자유.
8.them away — 대명사 목적어는 동사와 부사 사이에.
9.found out the technique — 명사는 found out + 명사 가능. 대명사면 found it out.
10.see me off — 대명사 목적어는 동사와 부사 사이에.

F-2
수여동사 · 어순 교정
다음 문장의 밑줄 친 부분을 바르게 고치세요.
1.His present cost 30,000 won from her. →
2.I envy your memorizing ability for you. →
3.Can you spare a few minutes to me? →
4.He wrote a birthday card for me. →
5.She made a cute ribbon of me. →
6.I bought my girlfriend them. →
7.You asked a few questions to me. →
정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · 바르게 고치기
1.→ her 30,000 won · cost는 간접목적어를 뒤로 못 보낸다. 「cost + 사람 + 금액」.
2.→ you your memorizing ability · envy는 전치사로 못 바꾼다. 「envy + 사람 + 사물」.
3.→ me a few minutes · spare는 4형식 어순 「spare + 사람 + 사물」.
4.→ a birthday card to me · write는 to를 취한다.
5.→ a cute ribbon for me · make는 for를 취한다.
6.→ them for my girlfriend · 직접목적어가 대명사(them)이면 3형식으로. 「buy + 사물 + for + 사람」.
7.→ a few questions of me · ask는 of를 취한다.

② lay
