Unit 31 · 부정사의 형태부정사의 형태·원형부정사
Unit 31 · 부정사의 형태

A. 부정사의 형태

1. 단순부정사 ‘to+동사원형’
I’m glad to meet you.
2. 완료형 부정사 ‘to+have+과거분사’
He seemed to have been ill.
3. 수동형 부정사 ‘to+be+과거분사 / to+have+been+과거분사’
My only wish is to be loved by someone.
She seemed to have been admitted to enter the college.
4. 대부정사 ‘to+(반복되는 동사구 생략)’
He may go home if he wants to (go home).
5. 원형부정사 ‘(to 생략) 동사원형’
She let me go to the movies.   I saw him swim across the river.
6. 진행형 부정사 ‘to+be+~ing / to+have+been+~ing’
He seems to be looking at me.   He seems to have been waiting for her.
7. 분리부정사 ‘to+부사+동사원형’
I agreed to calmly discuss the matter. (그 일에 대해 침착하게 의논할 것에 동의했다.)
8. 부정사의 부정 ‘not+to+동사원형’
I told my kids not to worry about that. (걱정하지 말라고 말했다.)

B. 원형부정사

1. 사역동사 (have, make, let, help)의 목적보어
He made me go to the university.   Don’t let your child play in the street.   I helped her (to) do her homework.
2. 지각동사 (see, look at, watch, observe, hear, listen to, feel)의 목적보어
We saw the space shuttle take off.   I heard Jim play the piano.   I felt the table tremble.
3. 관용구
had better 동사원형~하는 것이 낫다 would/had rather 동사원형차라리 ~하는 게 낫다 cannot but 동사원형~하지 않을 수 없다 may well 동사원형~하는 것은 당연하다 may as well 동사원형~하는 편이 낫다

Unit 32 · 부정사의 용법명사·형용사·부사적 용법
Unit 32 · 부정사의 용법

A. 명사적 용법

1. 주어
It is difficult to master English. = To master English is difficult.
2. 보어
My hobby is to collect coins. (주격 보어)   I believe him to be polite. (목적격 보어)
3. 목적어 — expect, hope, like, begin, promise, start, try, want, wish, afford의 목적어
I cannot afford to buy that car.
4. 의문사+to부정사 = 의문사+주어+(should)+동사원형
What to read is an important question. = What you should read is an important question.

B. 형용사적 용법

1. 한정적 용법 — ‘~하는, ~할’의 뜻으로 앞 명사를 수식
I want someone to love.   I have no chair to sit on. (명사가 전치사의 목적어일 때 전치사를 써 준다.)
2. 서술적 용법
① seem=appear/prove=turn out/chance=happen+to+동사원형 : He seems to be honest. = It seems that he is honest.
  that절이 주절보다 한 시제 앞서면 ‘to+have+과거분사’ : The rumor proved to have been false.
② come/get/grow+to+동사원형 : She came to know the truth.
③ be to 용법 : 의무 You are to finish it. / 예정 The clerk is to come. / 가능 Not a star is to be seen. / 의도 If you are to get pearls… / 운명 Bell was to get married.

C. 부사적 용법

목적 ‘~하기 위하여’(=in order to/so as to) · 결과 ‘~해서 결국 …하다’ · 원인 ‘~하게 되어서’ · 이유·판단근거 ‘~하다니’ · 조건 ‘~한다면’ · 형용사 수식 ‘~하기에’
I came to school to study. (목적)   The boy grew up to be a great scientist. (결과)
You must be silly to do such a thing. (판단근거)   The student is easy to please. (형용사 수식)
cf) 결과구문: so ~ that … can’t = too ~ to / so ~ that … can = ~ enough to = so ~ as to

D. 부정사의 의미상의 주어

1. 나타내지 않는 경우: 일반사람 / 문맥상 뚜렷 / 문장의 주어와 같음 / 문장의 목적어와 같음.
2. 나타내는 경우: ① 일반적으로 ‘for+목적격’ : It’s not easy for me to swim. ② 사람의 성질·특징 형용사(bad, careless, clever, cruel, foolish, honest, polite, rude, silly, thoughtful, wise 등)는 ‘of+목적격’ : Isn’t it selfish of you to eat the bread alone?

정답 펼치기
Answer
have stopped
stopped(과거)가 주절 seems보다 앞선 시제 → 완료부정사 to have stopped.
