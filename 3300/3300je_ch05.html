Chapter V · Unit 10 Adjective Use & Order제5장 형용사 · Unit 10 형용사의 쓰임과 어순
10-A1
형용사 찾기
EX
A-1. 다음 문장에서 형용사를 찾아 괄호 속에 쓰세요.
  1. He was proud of his son. [ ]
  2. I made a few mistakes. [ ]
  3. You'll play one of the main characters. [ ]
  4. We have many things to learn. [ ]
  5. Is Korea a developing country? [ ]
10-A1번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
  1. proud — 서술적 용법(보어)
  2. few — mistakes 수식
  3. main — characters 수식
  4. many — things 수식
  5. developing — country 수식(현재분사형 형용사)
10-A2
한정·서술
EX
A-2. 다음 괄호 속에서 어법에 맞는 단어에 ○표 하세요.
  1. He enjoys (an alone, a lonely) life in the country.
  2. Is he still (live, alive)?
  3. Have you read (the latter, the later) part of the play?
  4. Look at that (sleeping, asleep) baby.
  5. He is not (worth, worthy) his salary.
10-A2번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
서술적 용법만: alive, asleep, alone 등(a-형용사)은 명사 앞에서 수식 불가. 한정적일 땐 lonely·sleeping을 쓴다.
  1. a lonely — alone은 서술적만, 명사 수식은 lonely
  2. alive — 서술적 용법
  3. the latter — 후자의/뒷부분(순서), later는 시간
  4. sleeping — 명사 수식은 sleeping(asleep은 서술적)
  5. worth — worth + 명사 (worthy of + 명사)
10-B
the+형용사
EX
B. 밑줄 친 부분을 같은 뜻의 어구로 바꿔 쓰세요.
  1. Poor people are often generous to each other. [= ]
  2. Young people in spirit enjoy their lives. [= ]
  3. The judge asked an accused person to stand up. [= ]
  4. He always helped the disabled. [= ]
  5. Some people try to explore the unknown. [= ]
10-B번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
the + 형용사 = ~한 사람들(복수보통명사) / ~한 사람(단수) / ~한 것(추상명사).
  1. The poor — Poor people = the poor
  2. The young — Young people = the young
  3. the accused — an accused person = the accused
  4. disabled people — the disabled = disabled people
  5. what is unknown — the unknown = 미지의 것
10-C
형용사 어순
EX
C. 괄호 안의 단어들을 이용하여 문장을 완성하세요.
  1. I'd like to cook . [hot, something]
  2. I can't find with it. [anything, wrong]
  3. I'll buy fans. [Korean, these, beautiful, all]
  4. This is . [a, thing, strange]
  5. This hotel has the . [best, facilities, convenient]
  6. happened. [strange, nothing]
  7. She bought silver cases. [the, both, two]
  8. I'll show you . [special, something]
10-C번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
어순: all/both + the + 기수 + 성질·색깔·국적 + 명사. -thing/-body/-one/-where는 형용사가 뒤에서 수식.
  1. something hot — -thing + 형용사 후치
  2. anything wrong — -thing + 형용사 후치
  3. all these beautiful Korean — 수량-지시-성질-국적 순
  4. a strange thing — thing(한 단어)은 형용사 전치
  5. best convenient facilities — 최상급 + 성질 + 명사
  6. Nothing strange — -thing + 형용사 후치
  7. both the two — both + the + 기수
  8. something special — -thing + 형용사 후치
Chapter V · Unit 11 Numerals & Quantifiers제5장 · Unit 11 수사와 부정수량형용사
11-A1
수사
EX
A-1. 빈칸을 채워 문장을 완성하세요.
  1. 그는 30대이다. = He is .
  2. 수천 명의 선수들이 출발했다. = athletes started.
  3. 그녀는 나흘에 한번씩 그를 만난다. = She meets him day.
  4. 나는 1990년대에 태어났다. = I was born .
  5. 그녀는 40대이다. = She is .
  6. 수천만 명이 그 프로그램을 봤다. = people watched the program.
  7. 그는 2년에 한번씩 그의 고국을 방문한다. = He visits his motherland two years.
11-A1번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
막연한 수: tens/hundreds/thousands/millions of + 복수. 연대: in the ~s. 나이대: in one's ~s. 주기: every 서수 + 단수 = every 기수 + 복수.
  1. in his thirties — 30대
  2. Thousands of — 수천의
  3. every fourth — every fourth day = 나흘마다
  4. in the nineteen-nineties — 1990's
  5. in her forties — 40대
  6. Tens of millions of — 수천만의
  7. every — every two years = 2년마다
11-A2
분수 읽기
EX
A-2. 다음 분수를 읽을 때의 표기법을 쓰세요.
  1. 3/7 →
  2. 5 3/8 →
  3. 2/5 →
  4. 1/7 →
11-A2번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
분수: 분자=기수, 분모=서수. 분자 2 이상이면 분모 서수에 -s. 대분수는 and로 연결.
  1. three-sevenths — 3/7
  2. five and three-eighths — 5와 3/8
  3. two-fifths — 2/5
  4. a seventh / one seventh — 1/7
11-B
many/much
EX
B. 다음 괄호 안에서 어법상 알맞은 말을 골라 ○표 하세요.
  1. (Many, much) people gathered on the beach.
  2. The antique table costs (a lot of, a great number of) money.
  3. Do you have (many, much) friends in this village?
  4. Don't drink too (many, much) wine.
  5. Do you have (many, much) time to prepare for the conference?
  6. Korea needs (many, a great quantity of) natural gas, too.
11-B번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
many/a number of + 가산 복수, much/a great amount(quantity) of + 불가산, a lot of는 둘 다 가능.
  1. Many — people 가산
  2. a lot of — money 불가산(a great number of는 가산)
  3. many — friends 가산
  4. much — wine 불가산
  5. much — time 불가산
  6. a great quantity of — gas 불가산
11-C
few/little
EX
C. 다음 괄호 안에서 어법상 알맞은 말을 골라 ○표 하세요.
  1. (Few, Little) people understood what he was saying.
  2. We need (a few, a little) money.
  3. Do you have (a few, a little) friends in this village?
  4. He could eat (few, little) bread.
  5. I know (a few, a little) about it.
11-C번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
few/a few + 가산 복수, little/a little + 불가산. a가 있으면 "조금 있는", 없으면 "거의 없는".
  1. Few — people 가산
  2. a little — money 불가산
  3. a few — friends 가산
  4. little — bread 불가산
  5. a little — 불가산 대용(어느 정도 안다)

Jin-il looked very ________ and stammered. "I... I must buy a... a birthday present for my... my wife. But I... I don't have enough money to... to buy this necklace."

• When she gets (A)emotion/emotional, she can't stop crying. • That's why I go to a (B)medicine/medical school. • The (C)last/lastly step is making it a rule to keep a diary every day.
① emotion - medicine - last ② emotion - medicine - lastly ③ emotion - medical - last ④ emotional - medical - last ⑤ emotional - medical - lastly
