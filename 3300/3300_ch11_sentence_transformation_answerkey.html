01 · Negatives부정문 만들기
01-A
부정문 · Be동사
1번처럼 아래 문장들을 부정문으로 바꾸세요.
1.I am looking for him.
→
2.You are looking at me.
→
3.She is waiting for you.
→
4.We are waiting for him.
→
5.They are waiting for him.
→
6.She was singing a song.
→
7.There was a spring in the forest.
→
8.There were a lot of old chairs here.
→
9.Here are a number of pumpkins.
→
정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · 전환
1.→ I am not / I'm not looking for him.
2.→ You are not / aren't looking at me.
3.→ She is not / isn't waiting for you.
4.→ We are not / aren't waiting for him.
5.→ They are not / aren't waiting for him.
6.→ She was not / wasn't singing a song.
7.→ There was not / wasn't a spring in the forest.
8.→ There were not / weren't a lot of old chairs here.
9.→ Here are not / aren't a number of pumpkins.
📌 POINTbe동사 뒤에 not을 붙인다. There is/are, Here is/are 구문도 be동사 뒤에 not.

정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · 전환
1.→ I cannot / can't ride a bicycle.
2.→ You could not / couldn't ride a bicycle.
3.→ She may not be fifteen years old.
4.→ We should not / shouldn't have done it.
5.→ They would not / wouldn't climb the cliff.
6.→ We shall not / shan't go on a picnic tomorrow.
7.→ They will not / won't visit our new office.
8.→ He must not / mustn't read this.
9.→ He cannot / can't be Mr. Gong.
📌 POINT조동사 뒤에 not을 붙인다. 단, 강한 추측 「must be(~임에 틀림없다)」의 부정은 「cannot be(~일 리 없다)」.

03 · Wh-Questions의문사가 있는 의문문 만들기
03-A
의문사 · 주어를 물을 때
밑줄 친 부분을 의문사로 바꾼 후 의문문으로 고치세요.
1.John goes to Tokyo once a year.
→
2.You are riding a bicycle.
→
3.You were playing the piano.
→
4.I live in Denmark.
→
5.The cat is on the table.
→
6.He and his son are watching TV now.
→
7.The movie was very interesting.
→
정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · 전환
1.→ Who goes to Tokyo once a year?
2.→ Who is riding a bicycle?
3.→ Who was playing the piano?
4.→ Who lives in Denmark?
5.→ What is on the table?
6.→ Who is watching TV now?
7.→ What was very interesting?
📌 POINT주어를 물을 때는 「의문사(주어) + 동사 ~?」 어순 그대로. who/what이 주어면 동사는 단수 일치(is/was/lives).
03-B
의문사 · 목적어/보어를 물을 때
밑줄 친 부분을 의문사로 바꾸고, 나머지를 의문문 어순으로 고치세요.

→
2.You are riding a bicycle.
→
3.You were playing the piano.
→
4.I loved her in my school days.
→
5.She will take care of her niece.
→
6.He gave Tom a piece of cake.
→
7.He and his son are watching TV now.
→
정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · 전환
1.→ What do I have?
2.→ What are you riding?
3.→ What were you playing?
4.→ Who(m) did I love in my school days?
5.→ Who(m) will she take care of?
6.→ Who(m) did he give a piece of cake?
7.→ What are he and his son watching now?
📌 POINT목적어·보어를 물을 때는 의문사를 앞에 두고 나머지는 「(조)동사 + 주어」의 의문문 어순으로. 사람 목적어는 who(m).

📌 POINT의문사가 있으면 「의문사 + 주어 + 동사」 어순. 단, 의문사가 주어면(who did that) 어순 그대로.

Answer · 전환
1.→ Who do you believe did that?
2.→ Who do you think will volunteer?
3.→ What do you believe they are?

Answer · 전환
