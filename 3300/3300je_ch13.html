Chapter XIII · Unit 22 Perfect Tense제13장 동사의 시제 II · Unit 22 완료시제
22-A1
현재완료
EX
A-1. 주어진 단어를 사용하여 빈칸에 알맞은 말을 쓰세요.
  1. I lost my key, but I couldn't find it. I don't have it now. → I my key. (lose)
  2. The jet plane was on the runway five minutes ago. It is in the air now. → The jet plane . (take, off)
  3. She is driving a motor cycle. This is the first time. → She a motor cycle before. (never, drive)
  4. She is going to visit Korea next week. This is her first visit to Korea. → She Korea. (never, visit)
  5. The light was off a few minutes ago, but it is on now. → Someone the light. (turn, on)
  6. He began fixing this computer. Now he finished it. → He this computer now. (fix)
  7. Mr. and Mrs. Kim got married 10 years ago. They are still having a happy marriage. → Mr. and Mrs. Kim for 10 years. (marry)
22-A1번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
현재완료 have/has + 과거분사: 완료·경험·결과·계속. 과거에 일어나 현재까지 영향이 이어지는 상황.
  1. have lost — 결과: 잃어버려 지금 없음
  2. has taken off — 결과: 이륙해서 하늘에
  3. has never driven — 경험: 몰아본 적 없음
  4. has never visited — 경험: 방문한 적 없음
  5. has turned on — 결과: 켜져 있음
  6. has fixed — 완료: 방금 고침
  7. have been married — 계속: 10년째 결혼 상태
22-A2
for/since
EX
A-2. 괄호 안에 들어갈 어법에 맞는 어구에 ○표 하세요.
  1. I have been fixing this computer (for, since) this morning.
  2. He has stopped smoking (for, since) three years.
  3. He has worked in this factory (for, since) 2000.
  4. It (has rained, rained) yesterday.
  5. He (has gone, went) to Japan. He's not here.
  6. I (have lived, lived) there ten years ago.
  7. When (did you go, have you gone) to L.A?
  8. He (has left, left) school in 2005.
  9. I (have bought, bought) a diamond necklace last week.
  10. He (has played, played) baseball since he was an elementary school student.
  11. I (was, have been) in Seoul since 2003.
  12. I (was, have been) in Busan in 2002.
  13. I (played, have played) with Jin-il when I was a child, but I (didn't see, haven't seen) him (for, since) I left for Seoul in 1998.
22-A2번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
since+시점, for+기간. 현재완료는 명확한 과거(yesterday, ~ago, last~, in+연도, when~)와 함께 쓸 수 없다.
  1. since — this morning=시점
  2. for — three years=기간
  3. since — 2000=시점
  4. rained — yesterday
  5. has gone — 결과(가버림)
  6. lived — ten years ago
  7. did you go — When 의문문(명확한 과거)
  8. left — in 2005
  9. bought — last week
  10. has played — since ~
  11. have been — since 2003
  12. was — in 2002
  13. played / haven't seen / since — when(과거)/현재까지/since+시점
22-B
과거완료
EX
B. 괄호 안에 들어갈 어법에 맞는 어구에 ○표 하세요.
  1. The bus (has already left, had already left) when she reached the terminal.
  2. The bus (has left, had left) the terminal, and it is not there.
  3. When I arrived at her cottage, they (have finished, had finished) breakfast.
  4. He couldn't propose to her because he (has forgotten, had forgotten) the ring.
  5. I (have lived, had lived) in this city for two years. That is, I live here now.
  6. I lost my purse, so I (couldn't buy, hadn't bought) red roses for her.
  7. She bought a new backpack, since she (has lost, had lost) her bag.
  8. He (has not waited, had not waited) long before the bus came.
  9. We (didn't go, hadn't gone) far, when it began to rain.
  10. Six years (have passed, had passed) since he left for Europe.
  11. His mother gave him a watch, but he (lost, loses) it.
  12. I doubt if it (is, will be) fine tomorrow.
  13. Please wait until he (comes, will come).
  14. It (has been, had been) three weeks since we got married.
22-B번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
과거완료 had+p.p.: 과거 기준 시점보다 이전(대과거) 또는 그때까지의 완료·경험·계속. 시간·조건 부사절에서는 미래도 현재시제로.
  1. had already left — 도착보다 이전
  2. has left — 현재 없음(현재완료 결과)
  3. had finished — 도착 이전 완료
  4. had forgotten — 청혼 못한 이유(이전)
  5. have lived — 지금도 삶(현재완료)
  6. couldn't buy — 지갑 잃은 결과(단순 과거)
  7. had lost — 새로 산 이유(이전)
  8. had not waited — before came 이전
  9. hadn't gone — 비 오기 전까지
  10. have passed — since ~ 현재완료
  11. lost — 단순 과거
  12. will be — doubt if(명사절)의 미래
  13. comes — until 시간부사절→현재시제
  14. has been — It has been ~ since 현재완료
22-C
미래완료
EX
<보기> Susan goes to school every day. She gets up at 6 o'clock. She starts for school at 7 o'clock and arrives there at 7:30. School begins at 8 o'clock. She has lunch time between 12:00 and 12:50. School ends at 9 p.m. She arrives at home at 9:30. This is the same routine every day, so it will never be changed tomorrow.
C. <보기>를 읽고 주어진 단어를 이용하여 빈칸을 채우세요.
  1. She at 6 a.m. tomorrow. (get, up)
  2. She at home at 6:30 a.m. tomorrow. (be)
  3. She home at 7:15 a.m. tomorrow. (leave)
  4. She at school at 7:30 a.m. tomorrow. (arrive)
  5. She her lunch by 12:50 tomorrow. (finish)
  6. She out of school at 9:15 p.m. tomorrow. (get)
  7. She at home at 9:40 p.m. tomorrow. (arrive)
22-C번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
그 시각에 진행/발생하면 단순미래(will), 그 시각까지 이미 완료되면 미래완료(will have p.p.).
  1. will get up — 6시에 일어남(단순미래)
  2. will be — 6:30엔 아직 집(상태)
  3. will have left — 7시 출발 → 7:15엔 이미 떠남
  4. will arrive — 7:30에 도착(단순미래)
  5. will have finished — by 12:50 이미 끝남
  6. will have gotten out — 9시 종료 → 9:15엔 이미 나옴
  7. will have arrived — 9:30 도착 → 9:40엔 이미 도착
Chapter XIII · Unit 23 Progressive Tense제13장 · Unit 23 진행시제
23-A1
진행형
EX
A-1. 괄호 안에 들어갈 어법에 맞는 어구에 ○표 하세요.
  1. The water (boils, is boiling) now.
  2. Water (freezes, is frozen) at 0°C.
  3. Listen to the girls. Whom (do they talk, are they talking) about?
  4. Oh, not again! You (always lose, are always losing) the key.
  5. (Is she resembling, Does she resemble) her mother?
  6. I (hate, am hating) Tom. I mean it.
23-A1번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
진행형 불가 동사: 지각(see·hear), 감정·상태(like·hate·resemble·know), 소유(have·own). be+always+~ing=못마땅한 반복. 불변의 진리는 현재시제.
  1. is boiling — 지금 끓는 중
  2. freezes — 불변의 진리
  3. are they talking — 지금 말하는 중
  4. are always losing — 늘 잃어버림(못마땅)
  5. Does she resemble — resemble 진행 불가
  6. hate — 감정동사 진행 불가
23-A2
미래·과거진행
EX
A-2. 괄호 안의 단어를 시제에 맞도록 고쳐 빈칸에 써 넣으세요.
  1. A: Is it okay if I come at 8 o'clock? B: No, I care of my patients then. (take)
  2. At 3 o'clock yesterday, she in her office. (work)
  3. The exam already by the time we get to the school. (start)
  4. Don't phone me between 11 and 12. I a meeting then. (have)
23-A2번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
미래진행 will be ~ing(미래의 특정 시점에 진행 중), 과거진행 was ~ing, 미래완료 will have p.p..
  1. will be taking — 그때 진료 중일 것
  2. was working — 어제 3시에 근무 중
  3. will have already started — 도착 시점엔 이미 시작
  4. will be having — 그때 회의 중일 것
23-B1
완료진행형
EX
B-1. 괄호 안의 단어를 시제에 맞도록 고쳐 빈칸에 써 넣으세요.
  1. This morning it was fine. But the ground was wet. It before. (rain)
  2. My clothes are very dirty. I the basement. (clean)
  3. I the book I bought yesterday. But I it yet. (read) / (not, finish)
  4. Where have you been? you computer games all day? (play)
  5. He for her for 30 minutes before she came. (wait)
23-B1번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
완료진행형: 현재완료진행 have been ~ing, 과거완료진행 had been ~ing. 계속의 의미를 강조.
  1. had been raining — 오늘 아침 이전부터 계속(과거완료진행)
  2. have been cleaning — 지금까지 청소해 옴(현재완료진행)
  3. have been reading / haven't finished — 계속 읽는 중 / 아직 못 끝냄
  4. Have you been playing — 하루종일 해왔니?(현재완료진행 의문)
  5. had been waiting — 그녀가 오기 전 30분간 기다림
23-B2
완료진행 전환
EX
B-2. 문장의 뜻이 같아지도록 빈칸에 알맞은 말을 쓰세요.
  1. We played soccer yesterday. It started to rain an hour after we began playing. = We soccer for an hour when it started to rain.
  2. I started to make a doll for her a week ago. Now I didn't finish it and am still making it right now. = I a doll for her for a week.
  3. I started to live in Seoul five years ago, and I still live here in Seoul. = I in Seoul for five years.
23-B2번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
  1. had been playing — 비 오기 전까지 1시간 해옴(과거완료진행)
  2. have been making — 일주일째 만드는 중(현재완료진행)
  3. have lived — 5년째 삶(현재완료 계속)
