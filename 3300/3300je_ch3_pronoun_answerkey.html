Chapter III · Unit 06 Personal Pronouns제3장 대명사 · Unit 06 인칭대명사
06-A
격변화
EX
A. 다음 괄호 속에서 어법상 알맞은 단어를 고르세요.
  1. The old man asked my sister (me, my) address.
  2. Where does (he, his) live?
  3. Every day has (it, its) own meaning.
  4. I have a dog. (Its, It's) name is Spot.
  5. I like (his, him) very much.
  6. Hamsters are very cute pets. (It, They) run very fast.
  7. That bike is not (your, yours), but (my, mine).
06-A번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
격에 맞게 선택: 주격(he)·소유격(my/its)·목적격(him)·소유대명사(yours/mine). its(소유격) vs it's(=it is) 구분.
  1. my — 명사 address 수식 → 소유격
  2. he — 주어 자리 → 주격
  3. its — own 앞 소유격
  4. Its — name 수식 소유격(It's=it is 아님)
  5. him — like의 목적어 → 목적격
  6. They — Hamsters를 받음 → 복수 주격
  7. yours / mine — 소유대명사(= your bike / my bike)
06-B1
It의 용법
EX
B-1. 두 문장의 뜻이 같도록 빈칸에 알맞은 단어를 써 넣으세요.
  1. To exercise every morning is good for your health. = is good for your health to exercise every morning.
  2. Having a foreign pen pal seems very exciting. = seems very exciting having a foreign pen pal.
  3. Seeing him again made her embarrassed. = made her embarrassed to see him again.
  4. He appears to be disappointed. = appears that he is disappointed.
  5. There seemed to be more orphans in the city. = seemed that there were more orphans in the city.
  6. It took me five minutes to eat the sandwich. = took five minutes me to eat the sandwich.
  7. It takes 15 minutes for him to prepare for hiking. = It takes 15 minutes to prepare for hiking.
  8. It is time for you to go to church. = It is time that you to church.
  9. It is time for her to wash the dishes. = It is time that she the dishes.
06-B1번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
가주어 It(진주어 to부정사·동명사·that절), It takes 사람 시간 to = It takes 시간 for 사람 to, It is time that + 주어 + should/과거.
  1. It — 가주어(진주어 to부정사)
  2. It — 가주어(진주어 동명사)
  3. It — 가주어
  4. It — It appears that ~
  5. It — It seemed that ~
  6. It / for — It took 시간 for 사람 to
  7. him — It takes 사람 시간 to
  8. should go — (= went) It is time that + should/과거
  9. should wash — (= washed)
06-B2
강조구문
EX
B-2. 밑줄 친 부분을 강조하는 문장으로 고칠 때 빈칸을 채우세요.
  1. She went jogging last night. → was last night she went jogging.
  2. Bill bought a lot of candies yesterday. → was a lot of candies he bought yesterday.
06-B2번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
It ~ that 강조구문: 강조할 말을 It was와 that 사이에 둔다.
  1. It / that — It was last night that ~
  2. It / that — It was a lot of candies that ~
Chapter III · Unit 07 Indefinite Pronouns제3장 · Unit 07 부정대명사
07-A
one·other·another
EX
A. 다음 괄호 속에서 어법상 알맞은 어구를 골라 ○표 하세요.
  1. I need a pencil. Do you have (it, one)?
  2. They needed their own nicknames to play the game. So he made (it, one) up.
  3. I have two dictionaries. One is an English-Korean dictionary, and (another, the other) is a Korean-English one.
  4. Some are resting under the trees and (others, other) are having lunch.
  5. To know is one thing; to teach is (another, the other).
  6. I told the two girls that one would cut the cake and (others, the other) would select whichever half she liked.
  7. I keep a cat. I play with (it, one) in the room.
  8. Some students wore white caps, and (others, other) wore yellow (ones, them).
  9. Look at your fingers. Two of them are short, and (others, the others) are long.
  10. I've read the books you gave me. Do you have any (other, another) books that I can read?
  11. I've got three e-mails. One was from my parents. (Another, Other) is from my boyfriend. (Another, The other) is from Mr. Kim.
  12. We have a lot of things to drink. Coke is one thing. Coffee is (another, other).
07-A번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
one=같은 종류의 불특정, it=바로 그것. one~the other(둘), one~another~the other(셋), some~others / the others. A is one thing, B is another = 별개다.
  1. one — 불특정 연필(a pencil)
  2. one — 불특정 별명
  3. the other — 둘 중 나머지
  4. others — 불특정 다른 사람들
  5. another — A is one thing; B is another
  6. the other — 둘 중 나머지
  7. it — 내가 기르는 바로 그 고양이
  8. others / ones — 다른 학생들 / caps=ones
  9. the others — 나머지 전부(특정)
  10. other — 복수 books 수식은 other
  11. Another / The other — 셋: One~Another~The other
  12. another — 별개(one thing~another)
07-B
all·each·every
EX
B. 다음 괄호 속에서 어법상 알맞은 단어를 골라 ○표 하세요.
  1. All (is, are) excited about the movie.
  2. All that glitters (is, are) not gold.
  3. Both the men (like, likes) you.
  4. Solve each (question, questions) in this math book.
  5. Every (table, tables) has four chairs.
  6. Each boy has (his, their) own interests.
  7. Every (dog, dogs) has his day.
  8. The magazine is published every fifteen (day, days).
  9. The newspaper is published every seventh (day, days).
  10. I'll take (both, neither) part.
07-B번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
all=사람이면 복수·사물(everything)이면 단수, both=복수, each·every·either·neither=단수 명사+단수 동사. every 기수+복수(fifteen days)=서수+단수(fifteenth day).
  1. are — all=사람(복수)
  2. is — all that glitters=everything(단수)
  3. like — both=복수
  4. question — each+단수명사
  5. table — every+단수명사
  6. his — each boy=단수 → his
  7. dog — every+단수명사
  8. days — every 기수 + 복수(15일마다)
  9. day — every 서수 + 단수
  10. neither — take part(관용) → neither part
Chapter III · Unit 08 Other Pronouns & Partial Negation제3장 · Unit 08 기타대명사와 부분부정
08-A
재귀대명사
EX
A. 다음 괄호 속에서 어법상 알맞은 단어를 고르세요.
  1. Your presence (yourself, itself) was her happiness.
  2. We should love (ourself, ourselves).
  3. He didn't seem quite (him, himself) this morning.
  4. You must make your own way for (yourself, ourselves).
  5. Make (yourself, myself) at home.
  6. Your point of view is different from (my, mine).
  7. The door closed of (itself, themselves).
  8. She says love in (themself, itself) is higher than friendship.
08-A번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
재귀대명사는 주어와 동일 대상을 받거나 관용표현에 쓰인다. of itself(저절로) · for oneself(혼자 힘으로) · in itself(본질적으로).
  1. itself — Your presence 자체 → itself
  2. ourselves — we의 재귀 복수
  3. himself — seem oneself = 평소답다
  4. yourself — you → yourself
  5. yourself — Make yourself at home
  6. mine — from + 소유대명사
  7. itself — of itself = 저절로
  8. itself — in itself = 본질적으로
08-B
that·those
EX
B. that과 those 중 빈칸에 들어갈 알맞은 것을 써 넣으세요.
  1. The population of Daegu is larger than of Ulsan.
  2. for minorities argue that poor people's right should be protected first of all.
  3. The seasons in Argentina are the reverse of in Korea.
  4. who like borrowing dislike paying.
  5. You should try to make around you happy.
  6. To be or not to be: is the question.
  7. The temperature here is lower than of Taiwan.
  8. who want to go home early may do so.
  9. present were all disappointed with the result.
  10. The man was among resigned.
  11. There is no power greater than of truth.
08-B번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
that/those=명사 반복 회피(단수 that, 복수 those). those who ~=~하는 사람들. those present=참석한 사람들.
  1. that — the population(단수) 반복
  2. Those — those (who are) for ~ = 찬성하는 사람들
  3. those — the seasons(복수) 반복
  4. Those — Those who ~ = ~하는 사람들
  5. those — those around you = 주위 사람들
  6. that — that is the question
  7. that — the temperature(단수) 반복
  8. Those — Those who ~
  9. Those — those present = 참석자들
  10. those — those (who) resigned
  11. that — the power(단수) 반복
08-C
부분부정
EX
C. 두 문장의 뜻이 같도록 빈칸에 알맞은 단어를 써 넣으세요.
  1. Not both of you are allowed to enter the college. = of you is allowed to enter the college, but isn't.
  2. Not all the presents were for me. = of the presents were for me, but presents weren't.
  3. Neither of them is permitted to go home. = of them is permitted to go home.
  4. Not everyone watched the soccer game last night. = people watched the soccer game last night, but didn't.
08-C번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
부분부정(not all/both/every) → 일부만 부정: Some ~, but others ~. 전체부정: neither = not either.
  1. One / the other — 둘 중 한쪽만 허용, 나머지는 불가
  2. Some / other — 일부는 나를 위한 것, 다른 것은 아님
  3. Not either — neither = not either (전체부정)
  4. Some / others — 일부만 시청, 다른 이들은 안 봄

19. 다음 빈칸에 들어갈 말을 순서대로 연결한 것은?
• I have no pen. Can you lend me ________? • I have lost my purse. I must buy ________. • I have lost my wedding ring. I must find ________.
① one - one - one ② one - one - it ③ one - it - it ④ it - one - it ⑤ it - it - one
19번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
② one - one - it
아무 펜이나 one, 새 지갑(불특정) one, 잃어버린 바로 그 반지 it.
