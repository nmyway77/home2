Unit 17관계부사와 복합관계부사
A
Unit 17 · when
다음 문장들이 같은 뜻이 되도록 빈칸에 알맞은 단어를 써 넣으세요.
1.I can't tell the time at which he left the house.
= I can't tell the time he left the house at.
= I can't tell the time he left the house.
= I can't tell he left the house.
= I can't tell the he left the house.
2.It was Saturday when he proposed to her.
= It was Saturday he proposed to her on.
= It was Saturday he proposed to her.
= It was Saturday he proposed to her.
정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · 빈칸
1.which / when / when / time
2.which / on which / that
📌 POINT선행사가 시간이면 관계부사 when. 「전치사+관계대명사(at which/on which)」 = when = that. 선행사나 관계부사 중 하나는 생략 가능.
B
Unit 17 · where
다음 문장들이 같은 뜻이 되도록 빈칸에 알맞은 단어를 써 넣으세요.
1.Snow White entered a house in which there were seven small beds.
= Snow White entered a house there were seven small beds in.
= Snow White entered a house there were seven small beds.
= Snow White entered a house there were seven small beds.
2.Do you remember the park in which we had the first date?
= Do you remember the park we had the first date in?
= Do you remember the park we had the first date?
= Do you remember the park we had the first date?
= Do you remember the we had the first date?
= Do you remember we had the first date?
정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · 빈칸
1.which / where / that
2.which / where / that / park / where
📌 POINT선행사가 장소면 관계부사 where = that. 「전치사+which(in which)」로도 표현. 선행사·관계부사 중 하나는 생략 가능.
C
Unit 17 · 계속적 용법
두 문장이 같은 뜻이 되도록 빈칸에 알맞은 단어를 써 넣으세요.
1.I visited Chungnyeolsa, and I got married there. = I visited Chungnyeolsa, I got married.
2.She went to the village, and a famous artist lived there. = She went to the village, a famous artist lived.
3.We met at 7, and we left for Florida then. = We met at 7, we left for Florida.
4.I like spring, and I can see a variety of flowers then. = I like spring, I can see a variety of flowers.
정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · 계속적 용법
1.where — = and there(장소) → where
2.where — = and there → where
3.when — = and then(시간) → when
4.when — = and then → when
📌 POINT관계부사의 계속적 용법: when=and then(그리고 그때), where=and there(그리고 그곳에서). 앞에 콤마(,)를 둔다.
D-1
Unit 17 · how/why
다음 문장들이 같은 뜻이 되도록 빈칸에 알맞은 단어를 써 넣으세요.
1.This is the way in which I solved the question.
= This is the way I solved the question.
= This is I solved the question.
= This is the I solved the question.
2.That's the reason for which my grade is so low.
= That's the reason my grade is so low.
= That's my grade is so low.
= That's the my grade is so low.
정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · 빈칸
1.that / how / way
2.why / why / reason
📌 POINT방법: the way = how = that (단, the way how는 함께 못 씀). 이유: the reason + why = that = for which. 선행사·관계부사 중 하나 생략 가능.
D-2
Unit 17 · O표
다음의 괄호 안에 들어갈 알맞은 것에 O표 하세요.
1.There is no reason ( why / how ) he quit the job.
2.I didn't like the way ( how / that ) he treated her.
3.She told me the way ( that / how ) I can draw cartoons well.
4.I can't understand the reason ( in which / for which ) she burst into tears.
정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · O표
1.why — 선행사 reason(이유) → why
2.that — the way + how 불가 → that
3.that — the way + how 불가 → that
4.for which — 이유 → for which(= why)
📌 POINT선행사 the way와 how는 함께 쓰지 않는다(둘 중 하나만). 이유의 관계부사 why = for which.
E
Unit 17 · 복합관계부사
두 문장의 뜻이 같아지도록 빈칸을 채우세요.
1.Wherever she may live, I'll find her. = she may live, I'll find her.
2.Please come and see us whenever you like. = Please come and see us you like.
3.However much she eats, she never gains weight. = much she eats, she never gains weight.
4.Wherever you may hide, I'll find you in a minute. = you may hide, I'll find you in a minute.
5.However humble it may be, there's no place like home. = humble it may be, there's no place like home.
6.Whenever you visit me, I'll always be there waiting for you. = you visit me, I'll always be there waiting for you.
7.Please be seated wherever you like. = Please be seated you like.
정답 & 해설 보기
Answer · 복합관계부사
1.No matter where — Wherever(양보) = No matter where
2.at any time (when) — whenever(시간) = at any time when
3.No matter how — However(양보) = No matter how
4.No matter where — Wherever = No matter where
5.No matter how — However = No matter how
6.No matter when — Whenever(양보) = No matter when
7.at any place (where) — wherever(장소) = at any place where
📌 POINT양보: whenever=No matter when, wherever=No matter where, however=No matter how. 시간/장소: whenever=at any time when, wherever=at any place where.
