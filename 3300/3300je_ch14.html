Chapter XIV · Unit 24 Auxiliary Verbs제14장 조동사 · Unit 24 여러 가지 조동사의 용법
24-A
can
EX
A. 괄호 속에서 어법에 맞는 어구에 ○표 하세요.
  1. Two years ago, I (can't, couldn't) walk very well because of the traffic accident.
  2. She (can't, couldn't) run that fast last year.
  3. She (will be able to go, can be going to go) to the university.
  4. We (couldn't but, couldn't help) wait for her.
  5. They (can, can't able to) take a rest.
  6. She (must have been passed, can't have passed) the test.
  7. (Can, Should) the rumor be true? It's impossible.
  8. If you don't fill in this form, you (won't can, won't be able to) apply for this job.
  9. I want (to can speak, to be able to speak) English as fluently as you.
  10. She was a genius! She (could, couldn't) read when she was three years old.
24-A번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
can = be able to(능력). 조동사는 겹쳐 쓸 수 없어 미래·to부정사엔 be able to. cannot but + 동사원형 = cannot help + ~ing(~하지 않을 수 없다). can't have p.p.=~였을 리 없다.
  1. couldn't — 과거(two years ago)
  2. couldn't — last year
  3. will be able to go — 미래는 be able to
  4. couldn't but — but+동사원형 wait
  5. can — can't able to는 틀린 형태
  6. can't have passed — 과거 부정 추측
  7. Can — 강한 의심(설마 ~일까)
  8. won't be able to — 미래 부정
  9. to be able to speak — to부정사 뒤
  10. could — 과거의 능력
24-B
must
EX
B. 괄호 속에서 어법에 맞는 어구에 ○표 하세요.
  1. You must wash your hands. = You (have to, can) wash your hands.
  2. She (have to, has to) run as fast as possible.
  3. He (has to, can't) help me. He hates me a lot.
  4. I (will must, will have to) hand in my report by this Friday.
  5. You (must, have to) have followed her.
  6. I (must did, had to do) my best to win the championship.
  7. She (must, have to) study hard for tomorrow's test.
  8. She (must have to break, must have broken) the glass.
  9. I (must have been, cannot have been) out of my mind to make such a mistake.
  10. She's got to see the doctor now. = She (has seen, has to see) the doctor now.
  11. We (didn't must, didn't have to) wait long.
  12. A: May I go home? B: No, you (must not, need not).
  13. A: Must I come back before noon? B: No, you (must not, need not).
  14. You need not come here tomorrow. = You (must not come, don't have to) come here tomorrow.
24-B번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
must = have to(의무). 부정: must not=금지, need not = don't have to=불필요. 미래는 will have to, 과거는 had to. must have p.p.=~였음에 틀림없다.
  1. have to — must = have to
  2. has to — 3인칭 has to
  3. can't — 도울 리 없다
  4. will have to — 미래 의무
  5. must — must have p.p.(과거 추측)
  6. had to do — 과거 의무
  7. must — have to는 has to여야 함
  8. must have broken — 과거 추측
  9. must have been — 제정신이 아니었음에 틀림없다
  10. has to see — has got to = has to
  11. didn't have to — 과거 불필요
  12. must not — 금지(가면 안 된다)
  13. need not — 불필요(그럴 필요 없다)
  14. don't have to — need not = don't have to
24-C
may
EX
C. 괄호 속에서 가장 적합한 어구에 ○표 하세요.
  1. It is possible that she is asleep. = She (may, can't) be asleep.
  2. It is possible that he was in jail. = He (may, can't) have been in jail.
  3. It is certain that he knew the truth. = He (may have known, must have known) the truth.
  4. You (may well, may as well) get embarrassed.
  5. You (may well, may as well) take a rest.
  6. You look pale. You (may, can't) have been sick lately.
  7. He (may can, may be able to) use both hands well.
  8. May I go to the movies with Jill? - No, you (must, must not).
  9. May I sit here? - Yes, you (may, must).
  10. He (must, can't) have stolen my purse because he has my ID card.
24-C번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
may=허가/추측. may well=~하는 것도 당연하다, may as well=~하는 편이 낫다. may have p.p.=~였을지도 모른다. 조동사 중복 불가(may be able to).
  1. may — 추측(잠들었을지도)
  2. may — 과거 추측(may have p.p.)
  3. must have known — 확실 → ~였음에 틀림없다
  4. may well — 당황하는 것도 당연
  5. may as well — 쉬는 편이 낫다
  6. may — 아팠을지도 모른다
  7. may be able to — may can 불가
  8. must not — 강한 금지
  9. may — 허가
  10. must — 훔쳤음에 틀림없다
24-D1
should·would rather
EX
D-1. 괄호 속에서 가장 적합한 어구에 ○표 하세요.
  1. I'd rather (go, to go) home and take a rest.
  2. I insisted that we (go, would go) to the park right away.
  3. It is important that we (respect, will respect) the old.
  4. You'd (like, rather) try to appeal to the citizens.
  5. You (ought to not, ought not to) violate a rule.
  6. You (had better not, had not better) go there alone.
  7. I'd rather (go, to go) camping than stay at home for three days.
24-D1번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
would rather / had better + 동사원형. 부정은 ought not to / had better not / would rather not. 제안·주장·당위의 that절은 (should) + 동사원형.
  1. go — would rather + 동사원형
  2. go — insist that ~ (should) 동사원형
  3. respect — It is important that ~ (should) 동사원형
  4. rather — would rather try
  5. ought not to — ought의 부정
  6. had better not — had better의 부정
  7. go — would rather go than ~
24-D2
부정 전환
EX
D-2. 부정의 의미가 되도록 밑줄 친 부분을 고쳐 쓰세요.
  1. He may have stolen your money. → [ ]
  2. He may well be interested in that kind of business. → [ ]
  3. You may as well worry about it. → [ ]
  4. We ought to fight against our government. → [ ]
  5. You had better read the newspapers every day. → [ ]
  6. I'd rather go there. → [ ]
24-D2번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
부정어 not의 위치: may (well/as well) not, ought not to, had better not, would rather not.
  1. may not have stolen — may + not have p.p.
  2. may well not be — may well not
  3. may as well not worry — may as well not
  4. ought not to fight — ought not to
  5. had better not read — had better not
  6. I'd rather not go — would rather not
24-E
do·used to·would
EX
E. 괄호 안에 들어갈 적합한 어구에 ○표 하세요.
  1. The house (used to, would) have a blue gate.
  2. I (used to visiting, used to visit) her photo shop.
  3. I (do be proud of, do am proud of) my father.
  4. He (do likes, does like) to play the piano.
  5. Don't you like watermelons? - No, I (do, don't).
  6. (Don't be, Be not) silly.
  7. A: I'm going to buy her gloves for her birthday present. B: Oh, but I (do, did).
  8. I like listening to classical music, and so (do, does) Jane.
  9. I am used to (go, going) to bed so early at home and I always get sleepy so early.
  10. I am used to (walk, walking) to school.
  11. I (would, should) often go fishing in my college days.
  12. This small cleaner is used to (get, getting) rid of the dust on the keyboard.
  13. I got used to (drink, drinking) vegetable juice lately.
  14. He (will, would) play golf when he was in Thailand.
  15. She is not what she (was used to be, used to be).
24-E번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
used to + 동사원형=(과거의 상태·습관), would + 동사원형=(과거의 불규칙 습관, 상태 불가). be used to + ~ing=~에 익숙하다, be used to + 동사원형=~에 사용되다. do는 강조·대동사.
  1. used to — 과거의 상태(would 불가)
  2. used to visit — used to + 동사원형
  3. do be proud of — do + 동사원형(강조)
  4. does like — 3인칭 does + 원형
  5. don't — 부정 대답
  6. Don't be — 부정명령
  7. did — 대동사(나는 이미 샀어)
  8. does — so + does + Jane
  9. going — be used to + ~ing(익숙하다)
  10. walking — be used to + ~ing
  11. would — 과거의 습관
  12. get — be used to + 동사원형(~에 쓰이다)
  13. drinking — get used to + ~ing(익숙해지다)
  14. would — 과거의 습관
  15. used to be — 예전의 그녀
24-F
조동사+have+p.p.
EX
F. 괄호 안에 들어갈 적합한 어구에 ○표 하세요.
  1. It is possible that he bought a house. = He (may, can't) have bought a house.
  2. It is impossible that he drew the picture. = He (must, can't) have drawn the picture.
  3. It is certain that she took a shower last night. = She (must, should) have taken a shower last night.
  4. You didn't have to do it, but you did it. = You (needn't have to done, needn't have done) it.
  5. I had to prepare for the presentation, but I didn't. = I (must, should) have prepared for the presentation.
  6. She speaks Japanese fluently. = She (must, should) have been in Japan.
24-F번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
must have p.p.=~였음에 틀림없다, can't have p.p.=~였을 리 없다, may have p.p.=~였을지도, should have p.p.=~했어야 했다, needn't have p.p.=~할 필요 없었는데.
  1. may — 샀을지도 모른다
  2. can't — 그렸을 리 없다
  3. must — 샤워했음에 틀림없다
  4. needn't have done — 할 필요 없었는데(했다)
  5. should — 준비했어야 했다(안 함)
  6. must — 일본에 있었음에 틀림없다
