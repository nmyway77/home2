03-A번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
가산·불가산 구분이 핵심. 물질·추상명사(money, information, knowledge, bread, harm, wood, milk, furniture, mail)는 불가산으로 복수·a 불가. 집합명사 family는 단위=단수, 구성원=복수, people·cattle·police는 복수 취급.
  1. dogs / pet — 뒤 shop은 가산 단수 → a pet shop
  2. money — 불가산
  3. car
  4. three-story — 복합형용사는 단수형(-story)
  5. information — 불가산
  6. Knowledge — 불가산 → 단수 취급
  7. time — 여기선 "시간(불가산)"
  8. bread — 불가산
  9. harm — 불가산
  10. wood — 재료(불가산)
  11. money — 불가산
  12. hairs — "머리카락 개수"=가산
  13. ideas — a few + 복수
  14. room / people — room=공간(불가산), people=복수
  15. were — members=구성원 강조 복수
  16. is — family=단위 단수
  17. is — news 불가산 단수
  18. are — a number of + 복수 → 복수
  19. families — many + 복수
  20. are — clothes 항상 복수
  21. are — cattle 복수 취급
  22. milk — glasses of milk, 불가산
  23. a failure — "실패자(사람)"=가산
  24. pair — a pair of jeans

Chapter II · Unit 04 Idiomatic Nouns제2장 · Unit 04 명사의 관용적 용법
04-A
추상명사 관용
EX
A. 두 문장의 뜻이 같도록 빈칸에 알맞은 단어를 써 넣으세요.
  1. He is a man of wisdom. = He is a man.
  2. She looked like a woman of learning. = She looked like a woman.
  3. It was of no use to consider the matter seriously. = It was to consider the matter seriously.
  4. This book is of use to me. = This book is to me.
  5. He solved the question easily. = He solved the question ease.
  6. He arrived at the restaurant punctually. = He arrived at the restaurant time.
  7. I met him accidentally. = I met him accident.
  8. Do you want to talk with me privately? = Do you want to talk with me private?
  9. We should examine it carefully. = We should examine it care.
  10. They married hastily. = They married haste.
  11. He continues to visit us occasionally. = He continues to visit us occasion.
  12. You must work patiently. = You must work patience.
  13. She was very kind. = She was kindness .
  14. The girl was very happy. = The girl was smiles.
04-A번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
of + 추상명사 = 형용사(of wisdom=wise), 전치사 + 추상명사 = 부사(with ease=easily, on time=punctually, by accident=accidentally, in private=privately, with care=carefully, in haste=hastily, on occasion=occasionally, with patience=patiently). 추상명사 itself / all + 추상명사 = 매우 ~한.
  1. wise
  2. learned — 박식한
  3. useless — of no use
  4. useful — of use
  5. with — with ease = easily
  6. on — on time = punctually
  7. by — by accident
  8. in — in private
  9. with — with care
  10. in — in haste
  11. on — on occasion
  12. with — with patience
  13. itself — kindness itself = 매우 친절한
  14. all — all smiles = 매우 기쁜
04-B
기능 전환
EX
B. 우리말과 같은 뜻이 되도록 빈칸을 채우세요.
  1. 고래는 포유류이다. = are mammals. = whale is a mammal. = whale is a mammal.
  2. 개는 자신의 주인에게 충직하다. = are loyal to their owners. = dog is loyal to its owner. = dog is loyal to its owner.
  3. 그녀는 모성애가 솟아남을 느꼈다. = She felt mother rise in her heart.
  4. 사람이 가난해지면 거지 근성이 생겨난다. = When one is reduced to poverty, beggar will come out.
  5. 그는 그 순간 판사로서의 본분을 잊어버렸다. = He forgot judge at the moment.
  6. Mr. Cho라는 분이 얼마 전에 너를 만나러 오셨어. = Mr. Cho came to meet you before long.
  7. 그의 희망은 Einstein과 같은 훌륭한 과학자가 되는 것이다. = His hope is to be Einstein.
  8. Brown씨 부부는 어제 그 영화를 봤다. = Browns watched the movie yesterday.
  9. Johnson 가문은 그 도시에 많은 영향력을 갖고 있다. = Johnsons have a great influence on the city.
  10. 그는 한국의 고흐와 같은 위대한 화가이다. = He is Gogh of Korea.
04-B번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
종족대표는 복수 / a+단수 / the+단수 세 방식. the+보통명사=추상명사(the mother=모성애, the beggar=거지근성). a+사람이름=~같은 사람(an Einstein), the+성(姓)의 복수=~씨 부부·가족(The Browns), of 수식 있으면 the(the Gogh of Korea).
  1. Whales / A / The — 종족대표 3방식
  2. Dogs / A / The — 종족대표 3방식
  3. the — the mother = 모성애
  4. the — the beggar = 거지 근성
  5. the — the judge = 판사의 본분
  6. A — A Mr. Cho = ~라는 분
  7. an — an Einstein = ~같은 사람 (모음 → an)
  8. The — The Browns = Brown씨 부부
  9. The — The Johnsons = Johnson 가문
  10. the — of Korea 수식 → the Gogh
