Unit 25 · 3형식의 수동태3형식의 수동태
Unit 25 · 3형식의 수동태

A. 능동태를 수동태로 바꾸는 방법

1. 능동태의 목적어를 수동태의 주어로 한다.
He wrote this novel. → This novel was written by him.
2. 능동태의 동사는 「be+과거분사」 형태로 한다.
They love their teacher. → Their teacher is loved by them.
3. 능동태의 주어는 「by+목적격」 형태로 한다.
She built this house. → This house was built by her.
cf) 능동태의 주어가 일반인을 나타내거나 행위의 주체를 나타낼 필요가 없을 때는 「by+목적격」을 생략할 수 있다.

B. 수동태의 시제

시제능동태수동태
현재I clean my room.My room is cleaned by me.
과거I cleaned my room.My room was cleaned by me.
미래I will clean my room.My room will be cleaned by me.
현재완료I have cleaned my room.My room has been cleaned by me.
과거완료I had cleaned my room.My room had been cleaned by me.
미래완료I will have cleaned my room.My room will have been cleaned by me.
현재진행I am cleaning my room.My room is being cleaned by me.
과거진행I was cleaning my room.My room was being cleaned by me.

C. 목적어가 명사절인 경우

「It ~ that …」 구문으로 만들거나, that절 속의 주어를 수동태의 주어로 한다.
They say that he is a hero. → It is said that he is a hero. / He is said to be a hero.
cf) that절 속의 동사 시제가 주절보다 앞서면 「to have+과거분사」 형태가 된다.
People believe that she was guilty. → She is believed to have been guilty.

D. 동사구의 수동태

laugh at, listen to, look after, look up to, look down on, do away with, make fun of, make a fool of, run over, look into … 같은 동사구는 수동태에서 한 단어처럼 취급한다.
They laughed at me. → I was laughed at by them.
All of them look up to him. → He is looked up to by all of them.

Unit 26 · 4·5형식의 수동태

A. 4형식의 수동태

1. 간접목적어·직접목적어가 둘 다 수동태 주어가 될 수 있는 동사: give, hand, lend, offer, pay, teach, show, promise, tell → 간접목적어 앞에 to. ask → 간접목적어 앞에 of.
She gave me a chance. → I was given a chance by her. / A chance was given to me by her.
2. 직접목적어만 수동태 주어가 되는 동사: pass, read, sing, write → 간접목적어 앞에 to. build, buy, choose, cook, do, find, get, make → 간접목적어 앞에 for.
Mom wrote me a letter. → A letter was written to me.
He bought me a nice bag. → A nice bag was bought for me.
3. 직접목적어를 수동태 주어로 쓸 수 없는 동사: envy, save, spare.
I envy you your success. → You are envied your success by me.

B. 5형식의 수동태

1. 능동태의 목적어가 수동태 주어가 되고, 목적보어는 주격보어 자리에 온다.
They called the baby Snow White. → The baby was called Snow White.
You kept me waiting for an hour. → I was kept waiting for an hour.
2. 사역동사(make, help)·지각동사 문장이 수동태가 되면 목적보어인 동사원형이 to부정사가 된다.
He made me care for the baby. → I was made to care for the baby by him.
I saw a police officer enter the house. → A police officer was seen to enter the house by me.
3. 지각·사역동사의 목적보어가 현재분사·과거분사일 때는 형태가 변하지 않는다.
I saw him painting the house. → He was seen painting the house.
4. 사역동사 have와 let은 수동형으로 쓰이지 못한다.
She let me do the dishes. → I was let to do the dishes. (×) / I was allowed to do the dishes. (○)

Unit 27 · 주의해야 할 수동태

A. 의문문의 수동태

의문문 → 평서문 → 수동태 → 의문문의 단계로 고친다.
Did you write this novel? → Was this novel written by you?
의문대명사(whom, what, which)가 목적어: 「의문사(주격)+be+p.p.+by+목적격」
What did you do yesterday? → What was done by you yesterday?
의문사 who가 주어일 때:
Who discovered coffee? → By whom was coffee discovered?
의문사가 있는 의문문:
When did he take this picture? → When was this picture taken?

B. 명령문의 수동태

1. 긍정명령문 「Let+목적어+be+과거분사」
Open the window. → Let the window be opened.
2. 부정명령문 ① 「Don't+let+목적어+be+p.p.」 ② 「Let+목적어+not+be+p.p.」
Don't turn on the TV. → Don't let the TV be turned on.
Don't waste your money. → Let your money not be wasted.

EX
다음 타동사는 목적어를 취하지만 수동태로 쓸 수 없다.
escape(벗어나다) · cost((비용이) 들다) · resemble(닮다) · become(어울리다)

  1. × — escape 수동 불가
  2. × — cost 수동 불가
  3. × — resemble 수동 불가
  4. × — become 수동 불가

1
빈칸
My school ______ in 1965. It is famous for its soccer team.
① found
② founds
③ founded
④ was found
⑤ was founded
정답 펼치기
Answer
정답  ⑤ was founded
school은 설립되는 대상이므로 수동태. found(설립하다)의 과거분사는 founded → was founded. find-found-found(찾다)와 혼동 주의.
2
어법
다음 대화의 밑줄 친 부분 중 어법상 틀린 것은?
① A: I’m looking forward to the baseball game.
② B: It’s exciting to watch the teams compete.
③ A: Which team do you think will win?
④ A: I’m sure Korea can be beaten them.
⑤ B: I want Japan to win, anyway.
정답 펼치기
Answer
정답  ④ A: I’m sure Korea can be beaten them.
④ be beaten은 수동태인데 뒤에 목적어 them이 왔으므로 틀림. 능동 beat이 필요 → “Korea can beat them.”
3
전환
두 문장이 같은 뜻이 되도록 빈칸을 채우세요.
We believed that the kid had swallowed the coin. = It ______ that the kid had swallowed the coin.
정답 펼치기
Answer
was believed
명사절 목적어의 수동태 「It+be+p.p.+that ~」. 과거이므로 was believed.

② Her homework has been just finished with her.
③ Her homework had just been finished by her.
④ Her homework had been just finishing with her.
⑤ Her homework has just been finishing by her.

Answer

전환
〈보기〉를 참고하여 아래 문장을 바르게 고친 것은?
Koreans say that Dokdo is part of Korea.
① Dokdo is being said to be part of Korea.
② Koreans are said that Dokdo is part of Korea.
③ Dokdo is said to be part of Korea.
④ Koreans are said to be part of Korea.
⑤ Part of Korea is said to be Dokdo by Koreans.
정답 펼치기
Answer
정답  ③ Dokdo is said to be part of Korea.
that절 주어를 올려 「S+be said+to부정사」 형태로. Dokdo is said to be part of Korea. ②는 주어(Koreans)를 잘못 올린 오답.

영작

그곳은 그 유명한 여배우를 따라다니는 학생들로 가득 차 있었다.

어법
빈칸 (A), (B)에 들어갈 단어가 알맞게 짝지어진 것은?
All disorders are (A) by people (B) illegal things.
① make - commited
② made - committing

⑤ made - committed
