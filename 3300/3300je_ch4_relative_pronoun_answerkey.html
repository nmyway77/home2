Chapter IV · Unit 09 Relative Pronouns제4장 관계대명사 · Unit 09 관계대명사와 복합관계대명사
09-A
who 계열
EX
A. 빈칸에 who, whose, who(m) 중 하나를 써 넣으세요.
  1. Once there lived a farmer. He was diligent. → Once there lived a farmer was diligent.
  2. Once there lived a farmer. His wife was beautiful. → Once there lived a farmer wife was beautiful.
  3. Once there lived a farmer. All his neighbors liked him. → Once there lived a farmer all his neighbors liked.
  4. I talked to a boy. He was wearing a blue necktie. → I talked to a boy was wearing a blue necktie.
  5. I love the girl. Her hair is so long. → I love the girl hair is so long.
  6. I know a woman. She can type 300 words per minute. → I know a woman can type 300 words per minute.
  7. Is there anyone? That person will come to our rescue. → Is there anyone will come to our rescue?
  8. The students were very friendly. I know them. → The students I know were very friendly.
09-A번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
사람 선행사: 주격 who, 소유격 whose, 목적격 who(m). 관계절 안에서 그 대명사가 하는 역할로 판단.
  1. who — 주격(He→who)
  2. whose — 소유격(His→whose)
  3. who(m) — 목적격(him→whom)
  4. who — 주격
  5. whose — 소유격(Her→whose)
  6. who — 주격
  7. who — 주격
  8. who(m) — 목적격(them→whom)
09-B
관계대명사
EX
B. 빈칸에 들어갈 알맞은 관계대명사를 쓰세요.
  1. There was one thing. I don't like it. → There was one thing I don't like.
  2. This is a vase. I like the color of it very much. → This is a vase color I like very much.
  3. Is this the kid? You are looking for him. → Is this the kid you are looking for?
  4. Some people choose hamsters. They don't bite their fingers. → Some people choose hamsters don't bite their fingers.
  5. Many people buy pets. The pets will love them so much. → Many people buy pets will love them so much.
  6. There is a table. The legs of it need fixing. → There is a table legs need fixing.
  7. The people are in the square. You talked about them. → The people you talked about are in the square.
  8. She works for a company. It sells various goods for children. → She works for a company sells various goods for children.
09-B번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
사물·동물: 주격/목적격 which(=that), 소유격 whose(=of which). 사람: who(m).
  1. which / that — 목적격, 사물
  2. whose — 소유격(the color of which)
  3. who(m) / that — 목적격, 사람
  4. which / that — 주격, 동물
  5. which / that — 주격, 사물
  6. whose — 소유격(the legs of which)
  7. who(m) / that — 목적격, 사람
  8. which / that — 주격, 사물

09-D
what/which/that
EX
D. 괄호 속에서 알맞은 관계대명사를 골라 ○표 하세요.
  1. (That, What) is done cannot be undone.
  2. That's (which, what) I thought.
  3. Is this the last train (that, what) leaves for Busan?
  4. Don't postpone until tomorrow (what, which) can be done today.
  5. I know (what, who) I should do first.
  6. (What, Which) looks beautiful doesn't always mean that it's good.
  7. I bought her (what, that) she really needed.
  8. (What, Which) I like most is playing computer games.
  9. That's the idea of (that, which) I've thought.
  10. (What, Which) matters is that he doesn't like chicken.
09-D번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
what=선행사 포함(the thing which), 선행사 없이 명사절을 이끈다. 선행사가 있거나 전치사 뒤엔 which/that.
  1. What — 선행사 없음(주어절)
  2. what — 선행사 없음
  3. that — 선행사 the last train
  4. what — 선행사 없음(목적어절)
  5. what — 선행사 없음
  6. What — 선행사 없음(주어절)
  7. what — 선행사 없음
  8. What — 선행사 없음(주어절)
  9. which — of which(전치사+which)
  10. What — 선행사 없음(주어절)
09-E
what 관용
EX
E. 우리말을 영어로 고쳤을 때 빈칸에 알맞은 단어를 써 넣으세요.
  1. 그는 소위 걸어 다니는 사전이다.
    → He is is a walking dictionary.
    → He is we a walking dictionary.
    → He is a walking dictionary.
  2. 공원과 도시의 관계는 폐와 우리 몸의 관계와 같다.
    → Parks the city lungs the body.
09-E번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
what we/you/they call = what is called = so-called(소위). A is to B what C is to D(A와 B의 관계는 C와 D의 관계와 같다).
  1. what / called — what is called
  2. what / call — what we call
  3. so-called — 소위
  4. are to / what / are to — Parks are to the city what lungs are to the body
09-F
생략·전치사
EX
F. 빈칸에 알맞은 전치사나 관계대명사를 써 넣으세요.
  1. Mary is the student. You are looking for her.
    → Mary is the student you are looking for.
    → Mary is the student you are looking.
  2. There is a colleague. I want to make friends with him.
    → There is a colleague I want to make friends with.
    → There is a colleague I want to make friends.
  3. Do you know the gentleman? Your mom is talking with him.
    → Do you know the gentleman your mom is talking with?
    → Do you know the gentleman your mom is talking?
09-F번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
전치사는 문장 끝에 두거나 관계대명사 바로 앞에 둘 수 있다. 전치사+that·전치사+who는 불가 → 전치사+whom/which.
  1. who(m) / that — for가 문장 끝
  2. for whom — for + whom
  3. who(m) / that — with 끝
  4. with whom — with + whom
  5. who(m) / that — with 끝
  6. with whom — with + whom
09-G
계속적 용법
EX
G. 두 문장이 같은 뜻이 되도록 알맞은 접속사를 쓰세요.
  1. He offered her a good job, who gratefully accepted it. = He offered her a good job, she gratefully accepted it.
  2. He had three sons, who became teachers. = He had three sons, they became teachers.
  3. Many people call him a Midas, which he doesn't like. = Many people call him a Midas, he doesn't like it.
  4. She bought the car, which was not that expensive. = She bought the car, it was not that expensive.
  5. I happened to meet my first love, Sally, who didn't recognize me. = I happened to meet my first love, Sally, she didn't recognize me.
  6. I don't know the man, whom I have never seen before. = I don't know the man, I have never seen him before.
09-G번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
계속적 용법 「,+관계대명사」 = 접속사 + 대명사. 문맥에 따라 and/but/for/as/because 등.
  1. and — 그리고 그녀가 받아들였다
  2. and — 그리고 그들은 교사가 되었다
  3. but — 그러나 그는 좋아하지 않는다
  4. as / because — 그 차가 비싸지 않았기 때문에(이유)
  5. but — 그러나 그녀는 못 알아봤다
  6. for / because — 이유: 전에 본 적이 없어서
09-H
복합관계대명사
EX
H. 두 문장의 뜻이 같아지도록 빈칸을 채우세요.
  1. No matter what you do, you must do your best. = you do, you must do your best.
  2. I love Sam-soon and will do anything that I can do to keep our love forever. = I love Sam-soon and will do I can do to keep our love forever.
  3. I will hire anyone whom you recommend. = I will hire you recommend.
  4. Whatever has been said in Latin seems noble. = has been said in Latin seems noble.
  5. You can read whatever book you want. = You can read book you want.
09-H번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
복합관계대명사(관계대명사+ever): 명사절 whatever=anything that, whoever/whomever=anyone who(m). 양보절 No matter+의문사. 형용사적 whatever 명사=any 명사 that.
  1. Whatever — No matter what = Whatever(양보)
  2. whatever — anything that = whatever
  3. whomever / whoever — anyone whom = whomever
  4. Anything that — Whatever = Anything that(명사절)
  5. any / that — whatever book = any book that

ANSWER · 정답
④ in which
sleep in ~ → 전치사 in을 살려 in which. ① in 누락 ② 사물에 who ③ 선행사+what ⑤ in that 불가.

기출

________ half they choose, we shall be content with the other half.

기출

You must read a newspaper everyday, which will help you understand the world.
① though it ② but it ③ so it ④ if it ⑤ because it
18번 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
③ so it
신문을 읽어라, 그러면 그것이 세상 이해에 도움이 될 것이다 → 결과의 계속적 용법 = so it.

• Give the present to (A)whom/whomever you like. • My mother hates Mr. Brown, (B)who/which surprises me. • The man lived in Korea for three years, (C)who/which will help him to get a good job.
① whom - who - who ② whom - which - who ③ whomever - who - which ④ whomever - which - who ⑤ whomever - which - which
19번 정답 & 해설 펼치기

⑤ whomever - which - which
(A) to + 복합관계대명사 whomever(= anyone whom). (B)(C) 콤마 뒤 앞 문장 전체를 받으므로 which.
