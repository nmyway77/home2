PART A · 대화문 (D-1 ~ D-15)

D-1
자연스러움
1. 다음 중 대화의 흐름이 자연스럽지 않은 것은? (3점)
①A: It's raining cats and dogs. — B: Really? Then we should take an umbrella. ②A: This juice is on me. — B: Oh, thanks a lot! ③A: Spaghetti is not my cup of tea. — B: Great! Let's order more spaghetti for you. ④A: I feel under the weather. — B: Why don't you buy some medicine? ⑤A: I'll keep my fingers crossed for you. — B: Thanks. I have a big test tomorrow.
정답 & 해설
정답 ③
"not my cup of tea"는 "I don't like something"(좋아하지 않는다)의 뜻. 스파게티를 싫어한다고 했는데 더 주문하자는 응답은 모순.
※ 본문: Spaghetti is not my cup of tea. → It means "I don't like something."
D-2
유사표현
2. 밑줄 친 표현과 바꿔 쓸 수 없는 것은? (3점)
G: I'm in hot water.
B: Excuse me? Can you say that again?
①Can you repeat that, please? ②Pardon? ③What do you mean by that? ④Sorry? ⑤Excuse me?
정답 & 해설
정답 ③
밑줄은 반복 설명 요청(다시 말해 줘). ③ What do you mean by that?은 설명 요청(그게 무슨 뜻이야?)으로 기능이 다름. ①②④⑤는 모두 반복 요청 표현.
※ 함정 포인트: '반복 요청' vs '설명 요청'을 섞어 출제하는 것이 단골 유형.
D-3
유사표현
3. 밑줄 친 표현과 바꿔 쓸 수 없는 것은? (4점)
B: Not your cup of tea? What does that mean?
G: It means "I don't like something."
①What do you mean by that? ②Could you explain the meaning of it? ③Tell me what that means. ④I don't get the meaning of it. ⑤Can you say that again?
정답 & 해설
정답 ⑤
밑줄은 설명 요청. ⑤는 못 알아들어 다시 말해 달라는 반복 요청이므로 의도가 다름. ①~④는 모두 의미를 묻는 표현.
D-4
빈칸추론
4. 대화의 빈칸에 들어갈 말로 가장 적절한 것은? (3점)
G: I'll keep my fingers crossed for you.
B: Excuse me, but can you please say that again?
G: I said, "I'll keep my fingers crossed for you." It means " ."
B: Oh. Thanks. Have a nice trip.
①I'll pay for you ②I wish you good luck ③I don't feel well ④It's very easy ⑤You and I agree
정답 & 해설
정답 ②
keep one's fingers crossed = 행운을 빌다. 직후 "Thanks. Have a nice trip."과 자연스럽게 연결.
D-5
관용표현
5. 표현과 의미의 연결이 잘못된 것은? (4점)
①It's a piece of cake. — It's very easy. ②I'm in hot water. — I'm in trouble. ③We pigged out. — We ate a lot. ④We see eye to eye. — We can't agree with each other. ⑤Don't make a long face. — Don't feel sad.
정답 & 해설
정답 ④
see eye to eye = You and I agree(의견이 같다). "can't agree"는 정반대 의미. 부정어를 끼워 넣는 전형적 함정.
D-6
순서배열
6. 주어진 말에 이어질 대화를 순서대로 배열한 것은? (4점)
A: It's raining cats and dogs.

ⓐ It means "It's raining a lot."
ⓑ What does that mean?
ⓒ I said, "It's raining cats and dogs."
ⓓ Excuse me, but can you please say that again?
①ⓑ-ⓐ-ⓓ-ⓒ ②ⓓ-ⓒ-ⓑ-ⓐ ③ⓓ-ⓑ-ⓒ-ⓐ ④ⓒ-ⓓ-ⓐ-ⓑ ⑤ⓑ-ⓓ-ⓒ-ⓐ
정답 & 해설
정답 ②
못 알아듣고 반복 요청(ⓓ) → 같은 말 반복(ⓒ) → 의미 질문(ⓑ) → 의미 설명(ⓐ). 반복 요청이 설명 요청보다 먼저 오는 교과서 B. Match and Talk 순서.
D-7
내용일치
7. 대화의 내용과 일치하지 않는 것은? (4점)
G: I feel under the weather.
B: Excuse me, but can you please say that again?
G: I said, "I feel under the weather." It means "I don't feel well." I think I have a cold.
B: Oh. Why don't you buy some medicine before you get on the plane? You can get medicine at the store over there.
G: I guess I should.
①소녀는 몸 상태가 좋지 않다. ②소녀는 감기에 걸린 것 같다고 말했다. ③소년은 처음에 소녀의 말을 알아듣지 못했다. ④소년은 소녀에게 자신이 가지고 있던 약을 주었다. ⑤두 사람의 대화는 비행기 탑승 전에 이루어지고 있다.
정답 & 해설
정답 ④
소년은 약을 준 것이 아니라 저쪽 가게에서 약을 사라고 제안(Why don't you buy ~ at the store over there). 행위 주체를 바꾸는 함정.
D-8
빈칸추론
8. 대화의 빈칸에 들어갈 말로 가장 적절한 것은? (3점)
G: This juice is on me, Suho.
B: Excuse me? Can you say that again?
G: I said, "This juice is on me." It means " ."
B: Oh. Thanks a lot.
①I'll pay for the juice ②The juice is on the table ③I spilled the juice on me ④You should buy me juice ⑤I don't like the juice
정답 & 해설
정답 ①
be on me = 내가 사다(내가 계산하다). 직후 "Thanks a lot."이 단서. ②③은 on의 글자 그대로의 뜻을 이용한 함정.
D-9
상황적용
9. 표현을 사용할 상황이 적절하지 않은 것은? (4점)
①친구가 공연을 앞두고 있을 때 → "Break a leg!" ②비가 아주 많이 내릴 때 → "It's raining cats and dogs." ③시험 문제가 아주 쉬웠을 때 → "It was a piece of cake." ④친구와 내 의견이 같을 때 → "We see eye to eye." ⑤몸이 아파서 컨디션이 안 좋을 때 → "I'm in hot water."
정답 & 해설
정답 ⑤
몸이 안 좋을 때는 "I feel under the weather." in hot water는 '곤경에 처하다(I'm in trouble)'의 뜻.
D-10
내용일치
10. 대화의 내용과 일치하는 것은? (4점)
G: Thank you for everything, Jiho. I had a great time in Korea.
B: My pleasure. Please come visit me again, Lucy.
G: I'd love to, but before I do, I'd like to invite you to visit me in London.
B: Thanks. Anyway, it's too bad that you can't come to my soccer game tomorrow.
G: I'm sorry that I can't stay longer. I'll keep my fingers crossed for you.
B: Excuse me, but can you please say that again?
G: I said, "I'll keep my fingers crossed for you." It means "I wish you good luck."
B: Oh. Thanks. Have a nice trip.
G: Thanks. I'll keep in touch.
①지호는 런던에서 좋은 시간을 보냈다. ②Lucy는 내일 지호의 축구 경기를 보러 갈 것이다. ③Lucy는 지호를 런던으로 초대하고 싶어 한다. ④지호는 Lucy의 말을 한 번에 모두 알아들었다. ⑤두 사람은 앞으로 연락하지 않기로 했다.
정답 & 해설
정답 ③
I'd like to invite you to visit me in London. ① 한국에서 좋은 시간을 보낸 것은 Lucy, ② 못 감(can't come), ④ 반복 요청함, ⑤ keep in touch(연락하고 지내다).
D-11
빈칸추론
11. 대화의 빈칸에 들어갈 말로 가장 적절한 것은? (3점)
A: We see eye to eye.
B: Can you please say that again?
A: I said, "We see eye to eye."
B: What does that mean?
A: It means " ."
①You and I agree ②We are looking at each other ③We have good eyesight ④I can't believe my eyes ⑤We don't get along
정답 & 해설
정답 ①
see eye to eye = 의견이 같다. ②③은 eye의 직역 함정, ⑤는 반대 의미.
D-12
흐름판단
12. 대화의 흐름상 ①~⑤ 중 어색한 것은? (4점)
B: Everything looks delicious.
G: Yes. ① Would you like some of my spaghetti?
B: No, thanks. ② Spaghetti is not my cup of tea.
G: ③ Not your cup of tea? What does that mean?
B: ④ It means "I like something very much."
G: Oh, I see. ⑤ You don't like spaghetti.
① ② ③ ④ ⑤
정답 & 해설
정답 ④
not my cup of tea의 올바른 의미는 "I don't like something." ④처럼 '아주 좋아한다'고 설명하면 ⑤ "You don't like spaghetti."와 모순.
D-13
유사표현
13. 밑줄 친 문장과 의미가 같은 것은? (3점)
G: I feel under the weather. I think I have a cold.
①I'm so happy today. ②I don't feel well. ③I'm in trouble. ④I ate too much. ⑤I'm very lucky.
정답 & 해설
정답 ②
under the weather = 몸이 좋지 않다. ③ in trouble은 in hot water의 의미.
D-14
순서배열
14. 주어진 말에 이어질 대화를 순서대로 배열한 것은? (4점)
A: I'm in hot water.

ⓐ It means "I'm in trouble."
ⓑ I said, "I'm in hot water."
ⓒ Can you please say that again?
ⓓ What does that mean?
①ⓒ-ⓓ-ⓑ-ⓐ ②ⓓ-ⓑ-ⓒ-ⓐ ③ⓒ-ⓑ-ⓓ-ⓐ ④ⓑ-ⓒ-ⓓ-ⓐ ⑤ⓓ-ⓒ-ⓑ-ⓐ
정답 & 해설
정답 ③
반복 요청(ⓒ) → 반복(ⓑ) → 의미 질문(ⓓ) → 의미 설명(ⓐ). ①은 다시 말하기(ⓑ) 전에 의미를 묻는 순서라 부자연.
D-15
빈칸조합
15. 빈칸 (A), (B)에 들어갈 말로 알맞게 짝지어진 것은? (4점)
G: Thank you for everything, Jiho. I had a great time in Korea.
B: (A) Please come visit me again, Lucy.
G: I'd love to, but before I do, I'd like to invite you to visit me in London.
B: Thanks. Anyway, it's too bad that you can't come to my soccer game tomorrow.
G: (B) I can't stay longer.
①(A) You're welcome to ask. — (B) I'm glad that ②(A) My pleasure. — (B) I'm happy that ③(A) That's too bad. — (B) I'm sorry that ④(A) No, thanks. — (B) It is true that ⑤(A) My pleasure. — (B) I'm sorry that
정답 & 해설
정답 ⑤
(A) 감사 인사에 대한 응답 = My pleasure.(천만에) / (B) 더 머물 수 없는 아쉬움 = I'm sorry that ~ (= It's too bad that ~). ②는 (B)가 기쁨 표현이라 모순.

PART B · 어법 (G-1 ~ G-30)

G-1
계속적 용법
1. 어법상 옳지 않은 것은? (3점)
①She has two daughters, who are studying in France. ②I bought a dress, which was too small for me. ③This is Gimchi, which is a traditional Korean food. ④He created Hangeul, that is one of the most scientific writing systems in the world. ⑤My friend Linda, who is a chef, lives in London.
정답 & 해설
정답 ④ — that → which
관계대명사 that은 계속적 용법(콤마 뒤)으로 쓸 수 없다. 콤마 뒤에는 who/which만 가능. 목동중 단골 출제 포인트.
G-2
계속적 용법
2. 밑줄 친 부분과 바꿔 쓸 수 있는 것은? (3점)
The word shampoo comes from the Hindi word chāmpo, which means "to press."
①but it ②and it ③and which ④that ⑤so they
정답 & 해설
정답 ②
계속적 용법 = 접속사 + 대명사. 선행사(chāmpo)를 부연 설명하므로 and it. ⑤ they는 수 불일치, ④ that은 콤마 뒤 불가, ③은 접속사+관계대명사 중복.
G-3
용법구별
3. 밑줄 친 which의 쓰임이 〈보기〉와 같은 것은? (4점)
〈보기〉 The word robot comes from the play R.U.R., which was written in 1920 by a Czech writer, Karel Čapek.
①I like the bag which she made for me. ②The house which has a red roof is mine. ③Do you know which bus to take? ④Which do you like better, tea or coffee? ⑤I bought a dress, which was too small for me.
정답 & 해설
정답 ⑤
〈보기〉는 콤마 뒤에서 선행사를 부연하는 계속적 용법. ①② 제한적 용법, ③ 의문형용사(which bus), ④ 의문대명사. ⑤만 콤마+which 계속적 용법.
G-4
It~that
4. 밑줄 친 부분이 〈보기〉의 It ~ that과 쓰임이 다른 것은? (3점)
〈보기〉 It is interesting that the idea of using the word robot didn't come from Karel Čapek himself.
①It is certain that he quit his job. ②It was surprising that you returned so soon. ③It was Kim Yuna that I saw at the mall yesterday. ④It is believed that writing one's name in red is bad luck. ⑤It is true that he was the first president of the club.
정답 & 해설
정답 ③ — 강조구문
〈보기〉·①②④⑤는 가주어 It – 진주어 that절(that 뒤가 완전한 문장 + It~that 사이에 형용사/분사). ③은 It ~ that 강조구문: Kim Yuna가 that절 동사 saw의 목적어 → that절이 불완전.
※ 구별법: It과 that 사이의 말을 that절 안에 넣어 완전한 문장이 되면 강조구문.
G-5
관계사 종합
5. 어법상 옳지 않은 것을 모두 고르면? (정답 2개) (4점)
①Saturday is the day which I look forward to. ②Can you tell me the place at where we can meet to have a chat? ③I met my best friend, who is living in Seoul to follow his dream. ④This is the reason which he was late for school. ⑤Johnny Depp, who was in Pirates of the Caribbean, will make a guest appearance.
정답 & 해설
정답 ②, ④ (복수정답)
② at where ✗ → where 또는 at which (관계부사 앞에 전치사 중복 불가). ④ the reason 뒤에는 why / for which (which 단독 ✗). ① 전치사 to가 뒤에 남은 목적격 which ✓, ③⑤ 계속적 용법 who ✓.
G-6
본문 어법
6. (A)~(C)에 들어갈 말로 알맞게 짝지어진 것은? (3점)
The word robot comes from the play R.U.R., (A)[which / that] was written in 1920 by a Czech writer, Karel Čapek. In the play, robots are machines that (B)[look / looks] like humans. They (C)[design / are designed] to work for humans and are produced in a factory.
①which – look – are designed ②which – looks – are designed ③that – look – design ④that – looks – are designed ⑤which – look – design
정답 & 해설
정답 ①
(A) 콤마 뒤 계속적 용법 → which. (B) 선행사 machines(복수) → look. (C) 로봇은 설계되는 대상(수동) → are designed.
G-7
현재완료
7. 밑줄 친 부분과 현재완료의 용법이 같은 것은? (3점)
I have just finished my homework.
①I have been to the US before. ②I have lived in this city since I was in elementary school. ③She has already done the dishes. ④I have lost the key to the classroom. ⑤He has gone to Paris, so he is not here.
정답 & 해설
정답 ③ — 완료 용법
just/already/yet과 함께 쓰이면 완료 용법. ① 경험(before), ② 계속(since), ④⑤ 결과.
G-8
수동태
8. 어법상 옳은 것은? (4점)
①In India, the word was using for a head massage. ②The play made into a science fiction show in 1938. ③Robots is designed to work for humans. ④The hamburger invented in a small town in Texas. ⑤R.U.R. was written in 1920 by Karel Čapek.
정답 & 해설
정답 ⑤
① was used for, ② was made into, ③ Robots are designed, ④ was invented. 모두 '~되다'의 수동 의미 → be + p.p. 필수.
G-9
병렬구조
9. 빈칸에 들어갈 말로 어법상 알맞은 것은? (3점)
British traders in India experienced a bath with a head massage and   it to Britain in the 18th century.
①introduce ②introduced ③introducing ④to introduce ⑤introduces
정답 & 해설
정답 ②
and 앞의 과거동사 experienced와 병렬구조 → 과거형 introduced. it = a bath with a head massage.
G-10
영작 ★
10. 〈보기〉의 어구를 모두 한 번씩 사용하여 우리말을 영작할 때 ★에 들어갈 말은? (4점)
〈보기〉 ( comes / the Hindi word chāmpo, / from / which / means / "to press" )

"shampoo라는 단어는 힌디어 단어 chāmpo에서 왔는데, 그것은 '누르다'라는 의미이다."
→ The word shampoo ______ ______ ______ ★ ______ ______.
①comes ②from ③means ④which ⑤"to press"
정답 & 해설
정답 ④ which
The word shampoo comes from the Hindi word chāmpo, ★which means "to press."
콤마 뒤 4번째 자리 = 계속적 용법 관계대명사 which.
G-11
변환 개수
11. 계속적 용법을 「접속사+대명사」로 바꾼 것 중 어법상 옳은 것의 개수는? (4점)
ⓐ ~ chāmpo, which means "to press" → and it means ~
ⓑ ~ King Sejong, who invented Hangeul → and he invented ~
ⓒ ~ a dress, which was too small for me → but it was too small ~
ⓓ ~ the play R.U.R., which was written in 1920 → and which was written ~
ⓔ ~ my friend Linda, who is a chef → and she is a chef
ⓕ ~ huracán, which originates from a Mayan god → and they originate from ~
ⓖ I remain quiet, which makes people shocked → and it makes people shocked
①3개 ②4개 ③5개 ④6개 ⑤7개
