Answer
(1) without which   (2) which
(1) there could be no energy가 완전한 절이고 "그것 없이는"의 의미이므로 전치사를 포함한 without which.

(1)
Mary had a long vacation, which she spent many weeks in the mountains. ( )Mary는 긴 휴가를 가졌고, 그 중에 산에서 여러 주를 보냈다.
(2)
In the classes in that the teachers just stood and talked, or told everyone to read, he seemed to get bored and restless. ( )선생님들이 그냥 서서 떠들거나, 모두에게 책을 읽으라고 말하는 그런 수업에서, 그는 지루해 하거나 불안해지는 것 같았다.
정답 & 해설 펼치기
Answer
(1) X (which → during which)   (2) X (in that → in which)
(1) she spent many weeks in the mountains가 완전한 절이고 "그 휴가 동안"의 의미이므로 which를 during which로 고쳐야 한다.
(2) the classes를 수식하는 관계사절이다. 전치사 뒤에 접속사 that은 쓸 수 없으므로 in that을 in which로 고쳐야 한다.

선행사 + 관계대명사 + 불완전 / 선행사 + 전치사 관계대명사 + 완전

전치사 + 관계대명사 that은 불가능.   전치사 + 접속사 that은 불가능.

예외) in that + 완전: "~라는 점에 있어서"

Researchers studied two mobile phone companies trying to solve a technological problem. One company developed what it called a 'technology shelf,' created by a small group of engineers, (A)[which / on which] were placed possible technical solutions that other teams might use in the future. However, the other company proceeded with more seeming clarity and discipline, dividing the problem into its parts. Different departments protected their territory. Individuals and teams, competing with each other, stopped sharing information. The two companies (C)[did / were] eventually solve the technological problem, but the latter company had more difficulty than the former.

④on whichin whichwere
⑤on whichthatdid
정답 & 해설 펼치기
Answer ③
(A) on which   (B) in which   (C) did
(A) on which — were placed possible technical solutions(주어가 뒤로 간 도치)로 완전하다. "그 위에(on the shelf)"의 의미이므로 on which.
(B) in which — salespeople and designers were often included가 완전한 절이므로 in which.
