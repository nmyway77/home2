■ 지문 29 Topic: Literature · Human Experience · Timeless Art Form
Literature, in its essence, is a testament to the enduring power of human creativity and expression. It transcends time and space, connecting us with the thoughts and emotions of people from all walks of life, across centuries and cultures. Despite the rise of new technologies and the ever-changing landscape of media, literature has retained its relevance and significance as a timeless art form. One reason for literature's enduring power is its ability to capture and convey the complexities of the human experience. Through the written word, authors have the unique ability to explore the depths of human emotion, the nuances of relationships, and the challenges and triumphs of the human spirit. Literature allows us to see ourselves reflected in the pages of a book, to empathize with characters different from ourselves, and to gain a deeper understanding of the human condition.

Literature, in its essence, is a testament to the enduring power of human creativity, transcending time and space.

(A) One reason for this enduring power is its ability to capture and convey the complexities of the human experience.
(B) Despite the rise of new technologies, it has retained this relevance as a timeless art form.
(C) Through that ability, authors let us empathize with characters unlike ourselves and understand the human condition more deeply.

ANSWER · 정답
② (B)-(A)-(C)
📖 문제 해석
주어진 글 뒤 (A)(B)(C)의 순서를 정하라.
📖 단락 해석
(A) 이 지속적 힘의 한 이유는 인간 경험의 복잡성을 포착·전달하는 능력이다.
(B) 새 기술의 부상에도, 그것은 이 관련성을 시대 초월적 예술 형식으로 유지해 왔다.
(C) 그 능력을 통해 작가는 우리가 다른 인물에 공감하고 인간 조건을 더 깊이 이해하게 한다.
✦ 정답 도출 + 오답 함정 도입(지속적 힘) → (B) 'has retained this relevance'가 도입의 enduring power를 받아 유지를 진술 → (A) 'One reason for this enduring power'로 그 이유(능력)를 제시 → (C) 'Through that ability'가 (A)의 ability를 받아 구체적 효과를 설명. this relevance·this enduring power·that ability의 지시어 연쇄가 결정적. (C)를 앞에 두면 that ability가 받을 대상이 없어 오답.
