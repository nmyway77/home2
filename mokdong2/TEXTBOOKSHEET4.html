A·1 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
1 looking · 2 find · 3 help · 4 have · 5 trying · 6 searching · 7 show · 8 another · 9 anything · 10 different · 11 something · 12 take · 13 buy · 14 pay
물건 사기 1 (찾기): look/try/search for 모두 '~을 찾다'. help me find는 '찾는 것을 돕다', Do you have ~?는 '~ 있나요?'.

물건 사기 2 (다른 것 요청): show me something/another(다른 것을 보여 주다), anything else(그 밖에 다른 것), a different one(다른 것). 셀 수 있는 단수는 one으로 받는다.

물건 사기 3 (구매): I'll take/have/get it은 모두 '이걸로 할게요', pay for ~는 '~의 값을 지불하다'.

ANSWER · 정답
1. I'm searching for — look for = search for(찾다). ask for는 '요청하다'라 뜻이 다르다.

Hunger Marketing
Junho: What? The sale ends in two hours? If I won't buy the sneakers now, I will have to buying it at a higher price.
Stop, Junho! You're buying the sneakers just because you don't want missing the sale. You're fallen for a hunger marketing strategy.
If people can buy a product only for a limited time, they often feel "hungrily" for it and wants to buy it. About miss the sale, don't worry, Junho. You'll soon saw a similar sale again.
D·2 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER · 정답
won't → don't — 조건의 if절은 미래를 현재시제로 표현.
buying → buy — have to + 동사원형.
it → them — 복수명사 the sneakers를 받음.
missing → to miss — want + to부정사.
fallen → falling — 현재진행형 be + -ing(속고 있는 중).
hungrily → hungry — 감각동사 feel 뒤에는 형용사 보어.
wants → want — 주어 they(복수)와 feel에 병렬, 복수동사.
miss → missing — 전치사 About의 목적어는 동명사.
saw → see — 조동사 will 뒤에는 동사원형.

Hunger Marketing
Junho: What? The sale starts in two hours? If I don't buy the pants now, I will have to buy them at a lower price.
Stop, Junho! You're buying the sneakers just because you don't want to catch the sale. You're falling for a hunger marketing strategy.
If people can buy a product only for a free time, they often feel "full" for it and want to buy it. About missing the sale, don't worry, Junho. You'll soon see a different sale again.

ANSWER · 정답
starts → ends — 세일이 두 시간 후 '끝난다'.
pants → sneakers — 사려는 것은 '운동화'.
lower → higher — 지금 안 사면 '더 비싼' 값에 사야 한다.
catch → miss — 세일을 '놓치고' 싶지 않아서.
free → limited — '한정된' 시간 동안만.
full → hungry — 그것에 '굶주린' 느낌(헝거 마케팅).
different → similar — 곧 '비슷한' 세일을 또 보게 된다.
