01영영풀이3점
다음 영영풀이에 해당하는 단어로 가장 적절한 것은?
DEFINITION
to fall down suddenly, often after breaking apart
  1. ①crawl
  2. ②collapse
  3. ③swing
  4. ④react
  5. ⑤occur
정답 ②
collapse(붕괴하다) = to fall down suddenly. ① 기어가다 ③ 흔들리다 ④ 반응하다 ⑤ 발생하다. (I started to worry that the building would collapse.)
02영영풀이3점
단어와 영영풀이의 연결이 옳지 않은 것은?
  1. ①urgently — in a way that needs immediate attention
  2. ②violently — with great force
  3. ③panic — a sudden strong feeling of fear that prevents reasonable thought
  4. ④drill — a piece of furniture used for sleeping
  5. ⑤immediately — without any delay
정답 ④

PART Ⅱ · 본문 1 (3~6번)
One night in February, after I ⓐhad gone to bed, an earthquake hit. I woke up suddenly because my bed was shaking. I thought my brother was shaking my bed as a joke. But then I heard the mirror on my desk ⓑfall to the floor and break into pieces. I knew it wasn't my brother then, but I still didn't know ⓒwhat exactly was happening. Soon the whole room began to shake violently, and my confusion turned to panic. My mom shouted that it was an earthquake and ran into my room. Since it was my first time ⓓexperiencing an earthquake, I didn't know ⓔhow to react. I just kept saying, "What should I do?"

07어법 판별4점
윗글의 밑줄 친 ⓐ~ⓔ에 대한 설명으로 옳지 않은 것은?
  1. ①ⓐ는 지각동사 see의 목적격보어로, swing으로 바꿔 써도 어법상 옳다.
  2. ②ⓑ는 books를 보충 설명하는 목적격보어이다.
  3. ③ⓒ는 the glass를 뒤에서 수식하는 현재분사로, 사물이므로 covered로 고쳐야 한다.
  4. ④ⓓ는 지각동사 hear의 목적격보어로 쓰인 동사원형이다.
  5. ⑤ⓔ는 주절의 과거시제(started)에 맞춘 시제일치 표현이다.
정답 ③
분사 후치수식은 「사물=p.p.」가 아니라 의미상 능동/수동으로 판단한다. the glass가 사진을 '덮고 있는' 능동 관계이므로 covering이 옳다. ③처럼 기계적으로 covered로 고치면 비문. ①②④⑤는 모두 옳은 설명.

11관계사3점
윗글의 ⓐwho에 대한 설명으로 옳지 않은 것은?

12화법 전환4점
윗글의 밑줄 친 "Where are you?" my mom asked를 간접화법으로 바꿀 때 가장 적절한 것은?
  1. ①My mom asked him where are you.
  2. ②My mom asked him where he was.
  3. ③My mom asked him where was he.
  4. ④My mom asked him where he is.
  5. ⑤My mom asked him that where he was.
정답 ②
의문사 의문문의 간접화법: ask + 목적어 + 의문사 + 주어 + 동사 어순. 전달동사 asked가 과거이므로 시제일치(are→was), 인칭 변화(you→he). ③ 어순 오류, ④ 시제 오류, ⑤ that 불필요.
13어법 판별3점

<보기>

  1. ①ㄱ
  2. ②ㄷ
  3. ③ㄱ, ㄴ
  4. ④ㄴ, ㄷ
  5. ⑤ㄱ, ㄴ, ㄷ
정답 ③
ㄱ (O) 과거진행 was driving. ㄴ (O) pull over = 차를 길가에 세우다. ㄷ (X) to find out은 '알아보기 위해'라는 부사적 용법(목적)이다.

EARTHQUAKE SAFETY GUIDE
1. During shaking, get under a strong table and protect your head.
2. Do not use the elevator. Always take the stairs.
3. After the shaking stops, move to an open space away from buildings.
4. Stay inside the building until rescue workers arrive.
