📘 Lesson 4 · 학습 안내

의사소통고민·불만의 원인 묻기 — What's the matter? · 확신 표현 — I have no doubt that ~ 「~을 확신한다」.

문법 Aif / whether — 「~인지 아닌지」를 의미하는 명사절 접속사.

문법 B조동사의 수동태 — 「조동사+be+p.p.」 (can be used, will be fixed, must be returned 등).

📌 빈칸·선택지·어법 문제는 그대로 두고, 정답과 해설은 어두운 「펼치기」 패널 안에만 담았다. 먼저 스스로 풀어 본 뒤 펼쳐서 확인할 것.

Section A · Listen & Talk 1의사소통 · 대화
A-1빈칸·괄호Get Ready 2
빈칸을 채우고, 괄호 안에서 알맞은 말을 고르시오.
G:Mom, we've got a problem.
W:? ①
G:We're going to have dinner with Grandma this Saturday, right? But I just realized [that / what]② Sujin's birthday party is on Saturday evening. She's my best friend. [What should I do? / What I should do?]③
W:That [sounds like / sounds]④ a difficult decision. Let's talk about it with your dad.
G:Okay, Mom. I'd love to⑤ [see / seeing] Grandma, but I don't want to miss my best friend's birthday party.
A-1 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
① What's the matter · ② that · ③ What should I do? · ④ sounds like · ⑤ see
①What's the matter? 「무슨 일이니?」 — 고민·불만족의 원인을 묻는 표현.
②realized의 목적어절을 이끄는 명사절 접속사 that(생략 가능).
③의문문 어순 What should I do? 「나 어떻게 해야 하나요?」 조언을 구하는 표현.
④sound like + 명사 「~처럼 들리다」 (뒤에 명사 a difficult decision).
⑤I'd love to + 동사원형 (= I want to) → see.
A-2빈칸·괄호B. Listen and Choose
M:Junsu, are you okay? What's the matter?①
B:Hello, Mr. Smith. I have a problem.
M:What happened?
B:You know Jaewoo, Yunho, and I are best friends, right? They had a fight, and now I'm ②[stick] in the middle. I don't know ③ to do.
M:That [sounds / sounds like]④ hard. Do you know why they [had / have]⑤ a fight?
B:Yes, [but / and] it doesn't [sound like / sound]④ a big to me. I guess they [had / have] some kind of misunderstanding.
M:Why don't you⑥ all meet together and talk about it? I think they'll [listen to / listen] you.
B:That's a good idea. They're both good friends of mine. I can't ⑦take .
M:I understand. I hope everything ⑧[works / work] out.
A-2 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
② stuck · ③ what · ④ sounds / sound like · ⑤ had · but · had · listen to · ⑦ sides · ⑧ works
②stuck in the middle 「중간에 끼인」 — be stuck 수동 형태.
③what to do 「무엇을 해야 할지」 (의문사+to부정사).
④형용사 hard 앞은 sounds, 명사 a big deal 앞은 sound like.
⑤·but과거의 싸움이므로 had. 대조의 but, they had some kind.
⑦·⑧listen to 「~의 말을 듣다」, take sides 「편을 들다」, everything이 단수 주어 works.
A-3빈칸·괄호C. Listen and Speak
B:Hey, Mandy. What's the matter?①
G:I don't know ②what to do, Nick. My brother asked③ me to help him with his homework this Wednesday, but I told him I [can't / couldn't]. I have so many things to do④ that day.
B:What you have to do this Wednesday?
G:I need to go to the library to return⑤ some books. Then I have to meet you to work⑤ on our presentation. After that, I have to [prepare for / prepare] an exam at night.
B:Oh, you do have [a lot / a lot of] ④to do.
G:Yes. But my brother seemed⑥ down, so I feel bad.
B:Well, then how about⑦ meeting on Thursday instead [for / of] our presentation? Then you can do everything and also help⑧ your brother on Wednesday.
G:That would⑨ help me so much! Thanks for understanding, Nick!
A-3 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
can't · do · prepare for · a lot · let · of · out
시제현재 거절이므로 can't. 의문문 What do you have to do?
동사구prepare for 「~을 준비하다」, seemed let down 「실망한 듯 보였다」.
전치사「a lot to do」 할 일이 많다, instead of 「~ 대신에」, help me out 「~를 도와주다」.
A-4빈칸D. Talk Together
e.g.
A:What's the matter?
B:I need to prepare an exam this Thursday, I have to [go to / go] band practice.
A:It seems that you must choose between the two.
[추가 예시]
B:I need to go to a friend's birthday party this Friday, but I have to go to my brother's soccer game.
A:It seems you must choose between the two.
A-4 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
e.g. prepare for an exam ... but I also have to go to band practice. / 추가: but I also have to go ... It seems that you must choose.
표현prepare for(준비하다), but(대조), also(또한), go to(~에 가다), It seems that ~(~인 것 같다).
Section B · Listen & Talk 2의사소통 · 확신 표현
B-1빈칸Get Ready 2
G:Sports Day is coming. How do you want to help out?
B:I want to take photos for Sports Day.
G:That's great. You are really good at taking photos. I have that you will take some wonderful pictures.
B-1 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
I have no doubt that ~
확신have no doubt that ~ 「~을 확신한다, ~을 믿어 의심치 않는다」.
B-2빈칸·괄호B. Listen and Write ①
B:In the Send Our Stories project, we are going to make a picture book for children in other countries. We [divide]① everyone into three groups. Each group will responsible ② a different task. Group A will translate a Korean story into English. Group B will make drawings the book and edit③ it. ④After copies of the book [print], Group C will in of⑤ sending them the children. It won't be easy, but I have that⑥ the children who receive these books⑦ will really enjoy ⑧[them / it].
B-2 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
① divided · be responsible for · for · ④ are printed · be in charge of · to · no doubt · ⑧ them
①이미 나눈 일이므로 과거 divided.
담당be responsible for 「~을 담당하다」, be in charge of 「~을 맡다」.
④책이 인쇄되는 것이므로 수동태 are printed.
전치사·대명사make drawings for, send them to, no doubt that, 복수 these books를 받는 them.
B-3빈칸B. Listen and Write ②
B:I'm so excited about our museum field trip.
G1:Me too. Let's check our tasks. Yen, what you in of?
G2:I'm in of① taking pictures.
G1:Okay. Sejin, are you going to some research ② the museum?
B:Yes, I . Are you going to write our field trip report, Emma?
G1:That's right. I think we're ready.
G2:Good. I have that③ our project will turn ④ well.
B-3 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
are · charge · charge · do · on · am · no doubt · out
담당what are you in charge of? / I'm in charge of ~ 「~을 담당하다」.
동사구do some research on ~ 「~에 관해 조사하다」, 대답 Yes, I am.
결과turn out well 「잘되다, 잘 풀리다」, have no doubt that.
B-4빈칸·괄호대화 · Step 3
A:What do you want to do our class?
B:I want to make a student contact information list.
A:That sounds good. I have no doubt that it will be very helpful.
Step 3
Our group [choose] tasks [prepare] our class birthday party. I will buy a cake. Woojin and Taeho will decorate the classroom. Yeji will play birthday party songs. I have no doubt that our birthday party will be a lot of fun!
[추가 예시]
Our group [choose] tasks [prepare] our class flea market. I will put the poster the board. Junho will [prepare / prepare for] price tags. Minju and Taejun will display the items. ...
B-4 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
do for our class · Our group chose tasks to prepare for ~ · put the poster on the board · Junho will prepare price tags
전치사do for our class 「우리 반을 위해」, put A on the board.
동사과거 chose, 목적의 to부정사 to prepare for 「~을 준비하기 위해」, prepare(준비하다, 타동사) → prepare price tags.
Section C · Grammarif/whether · 조동사의 수동태
C-1빈칸POINT A · if/whether
빈칸에 「~인지 아닌지」를 의미하는 알맞은 말을 넣으시오.
e.g. A:What do you want to know?
B:I want to know my study plans are effective.
[추가 예시]
B:I want to know I will get taller.
C-1 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
I want to know if [whether] my study plans are effective. / I want to know if [whether] I will get taller.
if/whether「~인지 아닌지」를 의미하는 명사절 접속사로, know의 목적어절을 이끈다.
C-2배열POINT B · 조동사 수동태
주어진 단어를 바르게 배열하여 문장을 완성하시오.
1. This item (used / be / can) → This item in a variety of ways.
2. The elevator (will / fixed / this afternoon / be). → The elevator .
3. These books (be / by next Tuesday / must / returned). → These books .
C-2 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
1. can be used · 2. will be fixed this afternoon · 3. must be returned by next Tuesday
조동사 수동태「조동사 + be + 과거분사」 형태. can be used / will be fixed / must be returned.
C-3영작Real-Life Language A
그림을 참고하여 외출 시 확인할 사항을 if/whether로 쓰시오. [lock · close · turn off]
e.g.Before you leave, you should check if [whether] you turned off the tap.
1. Before you leave, you should check .
2. Before you leave, .

SAMPLE

C-4빈칸Real-Life Language B
괄호 안의 조동사와 <보기> 단어를 활용해 안내문을 완성하시오. [make · serve · order]
Jamil's Restaurant
Drinks 1. for free. (will)
Reservations 2. through the online system. (must)
The lunch special 3. only from 11 a.m. to 1 p.m. (can)
C-4 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
1. will be served · 2. must be made · 3. can be ordered
조동사 수동태음료가 제공되고, 예약이 만들어지고, 점심 특선이 주문되는 것이므로 모두 「조동사+be+p.p.」.

Section E · Do It Yourself & Culture심경·유형 고르기 · 어법 고치기
E-1심경 변화 · 빈칸Do It Yourself A
대화를 읽고 빈칸을 채운 뒤, 남학생의 심경 변화를 고르시오.
G:What's the matter? You look worried.
B:You know I'm going to be [give] a presentation on our team's research. But I feel very nervous when I talk in front of many people.
G:Oh, I didn't know that you got nervous. Actually, I also have a problem.
B:What's wrong?
G:I'm in of making the presentation materials, but I'm not good it.
B:You're ? I thought you good at it.
G:No, I'm not. Hey, then what about switching roles? I've given presentations many times before, so I don't get nervous at all.
B:Really? That would be great. I think I am good at making presentation materials, and I like making them!
G:That's good. Let's [talk to / talk] [the other / other] team members.
B:Cool. I feel so [relieve] now.
a. angry → pleased   b. hopeful → sad   c. pleased → worried   d. worried → relieved
E-1 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
빈칸: be giving · in charge of · good at it · You're not? · you were good · talk to · the other · feel so relieved / 심경: d (worried → relieved)
어법be going to be giving(진행), in charge of, good at, 부정 응답 You're not?, 시제 일치 thought you were, talk to ~, the other(나머지 모두), 감정 수동 relieved.
심경처음엔 발표가 걱정(worried)이었으나, 역할을 바꿔 안심(relieved)하게 됨 → d.
E-2유형 고르기 · 빈칸Do It Yourself B
빈칸을 채우고, Brian이 생각하는 Yumi의 리더 유형을 고르시오.
Brian:Why don't you run class representative, Yumi?
Yumi:No way. I've never thought about running.
Brian:Why not?
Yumi:Leaders have special qu. I don't think a person like me can [call] a leader.
Brian:What do you mean? You're really friendly and outgoing. You also help people along. I have no doubt that you will [elect] if you run.
보기: Green / Red / Purple / Orange / Yellow / Blue Leaders
E-2 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
빈칸: run for · qualities · can be called · help people get along · will be elected / 유형: GREEN LEADERS (Team Builders)
어법run for 「~에 입후보하다」, 명사 qualities, 수동 can be called, get along 「어울리다」, 미래 수동 will be elected.
유형친절·외향적이고 사람들을 어울리게 도와줌 → 긍정적 환경을 만드는 녹색 리더(팀 조직자).
E-3배열Do It Yourself C
주어진 단어를 바르게 배열하여 대화를 완성하시오.

B:Of course. When do you need it?
A:Can I get it this Saturday? It's my dad's birthday.
B:Sure. It (be / will / delivered) before noon.
E-3 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER

조동사 수동태케이크가 보내지고(can be sent) 배달되는(will be delivered) 것이므로 「조동사+be+p.p.」.
E-4어법 고치기Culture Link
[ ] 안의 말을 어법·문맥에 맞게 고치시오. (People Who Changed the World)
Mary Seacole was a nurse who helped soldiers [during / for] the Crimean War. She took care of [die] soldiers on sides and saved a lot of lives.

Nelson Mandela was South Africa's first black president. He spent his whole life [fight] the human rights of African people. He worked to make a society [where / which] everyone could live together in peace.
E-4 정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
for→during — 특정 기간(the Crimean War) 앞에는 during.
die→dying — soldiers를 수식하는 현재분사 「죽어가는」.
on __ sides→both — 「양쪽 진영」 on both sides.
fight→fighting (for) — spend+시간+(in) -ing 「~하는 데 시간을 보내다」, fight for 「~을 위해 싸우다」.
which→where — 선행사 a society를 수식하는 관계부사 where(완전한 절).
