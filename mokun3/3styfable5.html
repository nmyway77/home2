본문 「Waking Up to an Earthquake」 기반 · 이중조건 어법(문맥+어법) · T/F · 영어발문 · 학생반응 · 어법 개수형 — 목운중 현행 고배점 구간

[11~30] 다음 글을 읽고 물음에 답하시오. — Waking Up to an Earthquake

One night in February, after I had gone to bed, an earthquake hit. I woke up suddenly because my bed was shaking. I thought my brother was shaking my bed as a joke. But then I heard the mirror on my desk fall to the floor and break into pieces. I knew it wasn't my brother then, but I still didn't know what exactly was happening. Soon the whole room began to shake violently, and my confusion turned to panic. My mom shouted that it was an earthquake and ran into my room. Since it was my first time experiencing an earthquake, I didn't know how to react.

My mom pulled me and my brother out of bed. We ran to the kitchen and crawled under the table. I could see the light swinging violently and books falling to the floor. Our family picture dropped from the wall and the glass covering it broke. Every second, I could hear something else in the apartment break. I started to worry that the building would collapse.

Then the shaking seemed to stop. At that moment, my mom's cell phone rang. It was my dad, who was coming home from work. He shouted, "It stopped! Get out of the building! Take the stairs! Don't take the elevator!" My dad said he was driving home when the shaking started, but he pulled over immediately and was listening to the radio to find out what was going on.

We nervously made our way down the stairs and outside. Parts of buildings had fallen and had smashed several cars. We went to an open space to avoid more falling pieces. Although I had done many earthquake drills in school, I had never thought I'd experience a real earthquake. After that night, I began to take earthquake drills seriously. I realized that I should be prepared for the next earthquake, which can occur at any time.

(A)~(C)의 각 괄호 안에서 문맥과 어법상 올바른 것으로만 짝지은 것은? (3점)
이중조건
One night in February, after I (A) [had gone / have gone] to bed, an earthquake hit.
(B) [Since / Although] it was my first time experiencing an earthquake, I didn't know how to react.
I still didn't know (C) [what exactly was happening / what exactly happening was].
  1. (A) had gone — (B) Although — (C) what exactly was happening
  2. (A) had gone — (B) Since — (C) what exactly was happening
  3. (A) have gone — (B) Since — (C) what exactly was happening
  4. (A) had gone — (B) Since — (C) what exactly happening was
  5. (A) have gone — (B) Although — (C) what exactly happening was
정답 & 해설 펼치기
정답: ②
(A) 지진 발생(과거)보다 먼저 잠자리에 든 일 = 과거완료 had gone. (B) "처음이라서 → 어찌할 줄 몰랐다"는 이유(Since). Although(양보)는 어법상 가능해도 문맥상 비문 — 목운중 이중조건 함정의 핵심. (C) 간접의문문에서 의문사 what이 주어이므로 의문사+동사 어순 그대로 유지.
