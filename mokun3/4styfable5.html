내용일치

M: That sounds hard. Do you know why they had a fight?
B: Yes, but it doesn't sound like a big deal to me. I guess they had some kind of misunderstanding.

  4. Smith 선생님은 준수에게 둘 중 한 명의 편을 들라고 조언했다.

본문 「We Are All Leaders」 기반 · 조동사 수동태 · if/whether 명사절 · 리더 유형 적용형 · T/F · 영어발문 · 개수형 — 리더십은 목운중 1·2학기 연속 출제 핵심 본문

[11~30] 다음 글을 읽고 물음에 답하시오. — We Are All Leaders

Brian: The election is coming up. Why don't you run for class representative, Yumi?
Yumi: No way. I'm not the right person for that position. I've never thought about running.
Brian: What do you mean? I think you have very good leadership qualities. You're really friendly and outgoing. You also help people get along. I have no doubt that you will be elected if you run.

(Yumi's diary) Brian told me this afternoon that I have good leadership qualities. No one has ever told me that before. When he said that to me, however, I started to think. Can I really become a leader? I think leaders should have a vision, clear goals, and the ability to motivate others. I don't have any of those things. Then I suddenly started to wonder if these are the only qualities that make a good leader. Maybe I'm wrong. So I decided to do some research online.

Here's what I found! GREEN "Team Builders": ensure that the team feels valued · create a positive environment · are friendly and easy to talk to / RED "Logical Analysts": have good reasoning skills · analyze problems and situations · think of the most effective ways to achieve the team's goals / PURPLE "Hands-Off Managers": allow others to work on their own · do not try to control people · give advice only when it is needed / ORANGE "Strict Directors": make everyone's role clear · make sure everything is finished on time / YELLOW "Quiet Supporters": lead by example · let the team members shine instead · meet the team members' needs / BLUE "Creative Thinkers": approach problems in new ways · come up with fresh ideas

I was surprised that there are actually many different leadership styles, but soon I realized the reason. We belong to many different groups, and many different situations can come up in our lives. They all call for different leadership styles. Each group's unique situation determines the best leadership style. After reading everything, I became more confident. I discovered that I have some of the qualities of a "green leader." If my classmates think a green leader would make our class better, they might pick me to be class representative! Okay, let's try it!

(A)~(C)의 각 괄호 안에서 문맥과 어법상 올바른 것으로만 짝지은 것은? (3점)
이중조건
(A) I don't think a person like me can be [called / calling] a leader.
(B) I suddenly started to wonder [if / that] these are the only qualities that make a good leader.
(C) These are the only qualities [that / what] make a good leader.
  1. (A) called — (B) if — (C) that
  2. (A) calling — (B) if — (C) that
  3. (A) called — (B) that — (C) what
  4. (A) called — (B) if — (C) what
  5. (A) calling — (B) that — (C) that
정답 & 해설 펼치기
정답: ①
