Variation Blueprint

원본 대비 변형 포인트 (교사용)

  • 어법 오류 3곳 (9번): ⓐ could 뒤 leading → lead / ⓒ that → what / ⓔ making → makes
  • 연결어 3곳 빈칸화 (5번): However / Suddenly / Then
  • 신규 문단 추가: 5년 아르바이트 + 가정법 (11번 숫자 함정)
  • 반의어 치환: succeed ↔ fail / stable ↔ unstable / be bold ↔ avoid bold (7·10·11번)
  • 수상 정보 변형: first → second prize (11번)
Find a Dream and Live It!
David's Past and the Turning Point

Hello, everyone. My name is David Parker, and I'm a photographer. Today, I'm going to tell you how I found my dream and realized it. I hope my story can inspire you.

When I was young, I loved stars. I also liked taking pictures. However, I never thought these things could ⓐleading to a job. In fact, I didn't have a dream at all.

When I had to decide on a major in college, I chose engineering. Being an engineer looked OK. After college, I got a job at an engineering company. It was a stable job, but I didn't know ___(A)___ I really enjoyed it.

___(B)___, everything changed when I went on vacation to Iceland one winter. There I got a chance to see the Northern Lights. The lights were amazing, and I took many pictures of the dancing lights in the sky. For the first time in many years, I could feel my heart beating fast.

Decision and Change

After I came back, I entered a photo contest with the pictures I took in Iceland. Surprisingly, I won first prize, and this gave me a chance to think about my life. I realized ⓑthat taking pictures made me happy.

___(C)___, I wanted to become a good photographer, so I started to learn more about photography. After years of trial and error, I got better, and I began to do some part-time work as a photographer.

For about five years, I kept shooting photos on weekends while still working at the company. If I had not joined that photo contest, I probably ⓒwould never have realized that photography was my true passion. Sometimes, a single experience can completely change one's direction in life.

___(D)___ one day, I made a bold decision. I quit my job and decided to take pictures ⓓfor a living. I wasn't sure if I could succeed, but I decided to try. I really wanted to do something that made me happy. Now, I'm a professional photographer, and I'm happy.

Three Pieces of Advice

So do you want to find a dream and realize it? Here's some advice to help you.

First, follow your heart. Think about what you like to do and ⓔwhat making you happy. In my case, it was taking pictures of stars.

Second, work hard. Pursuing a dream is not easy. I became a photographer through hard work.

Third, be bold. You need courage to make decisions that will change your life. I was afraid but I took a chance. I truly hope you can find a dream, pursue it, and live it!

04
빈칸 (A) 접속사
3점
빈칸 (A)에 들어갈 말로 가장 적절한 것은?
It was a stable job, but I didn't know ___(A)___ I really enjoyed it.
  • ①whether
  • ②because
  • ③although
  • ④unless
  • ⑤since
정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
정답: ① whether
풀이. "정말 즐기고 있는지 아닌지" 알 수 없었다 → whether/if 명사절 접속사. know의 목적어절.
🔑 whether = ~인지 아닌지. didn't know / wonder / ask 뒤에 자주 등장. if와 대부분 교체 가능.
05
연결사 (B)(C)(D)
4점
빈칸 (B), (C), (D)에 들어갈 말로 가장 적절한 것은?
　(B)(C)(D)
①HoweverSuddenlyThen
②ThereforeSlowlyBefore
③For exampleFinallyAfter
④MoreoverSurprisinglyMeanwhile
⑤OtherwiseFortunatelyAlthough
정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
정답: ① However / Suddenly / Then
(B) 안정 직업 즐기는지 모름 ↔ 아이슬란드에서 모든 것이 변함 → 역접 However.
(C) 1등 수상 직후 급격한 감정 변화 → Suddenly.
(D) 시간 흐름 후 결정적 순간 → Then one day.

어법 틀린 개수
5점
밑줄 친 ⓐ~ⓔ 중, 어법상 틀린 것의 개수는?
ⓐ~ⓔ 복기
ⓐ I never thought these things could leading to a job.
ⓑ I realized that taking pictures made me happy.
ⓒ I probably would never have realized that photography was my true passion.
ⓓ I decided to take pictures for a living.
ⓔ Think about what you like to do and what making you happy.
  • ①1개
  • ②2개
  • ③3개
  • ④4개
  • ⑤5개
정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
정답: ② 2개 (ⓐ, ⓔ)
  • ⓐ🚨 could + 동사원형. leading → lead.
  • ⓑrealize + that절 ☑️
  • ⓒ가정법 과거완료 "If I had not joined, I would never have realized" ☑️
  • ⓓfor a living = 생계 수단 관용구 ☑️
  • ⓔ🚨 what + makes. what이 주어이면 단수 동사 → "what makes you happy". 동명사 making(×) / what 뒤 S+V 구조가 와야 함.
🔑 조동사 뒤 동사원형 + what절 주어+동사 일치.

영영풀이 옳지 않은 것
3점
단어 영영풀이로 옳지 않은 것은?
  • ①bold: confident and not afraid
  • ②pursue: to work hard in order to achieve something
  • ③stable: changing very often, with many ups and downs
  • ④inspire: to make you feel that you want to achieve something
  • ⑤courage: the ability to do something that frightens one
정답 & 해설 펼치기
ANSWER
정답: ③ stable
풀이. stable = 안정적인 (큰 변화 없이 유지되는). 선지는 "자주 변하는"으로 unstable의 정의. 반의어 치환 함정.

→ You ① ② ③ ④ ⑤ ★ ⑦ ⑧ ⑨ ⑩ ⑪.
